Translucent mushrooms found in the jungles of [[Continental Stark]], resembling the moon in appearance — some broken into half-moon or crescent shapes. Their fragrance is intensely inviting, triggering deep instinctive cravings that lure creatures toward them.

The mushrooms are hallucinogenic and would blind consumers with hallucinations if ingested. [[Ceril]] identified them as a form of poison that can be harvested and dried for later use. [[Kerben]] has expressed interest in developing them into battle poisons alongside wyvern gland extracts.
