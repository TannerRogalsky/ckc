A hole in the sky that the party aims to find and plug. [[Lyngbakr]] fell through it from [[Arcadia]]. One of the party's main quests.

In session 013, [[Sigil]] identified [[Emperor Shen]], an earlier practitioner of [[Touching the Sky]], as the party's greatest enemy. This suggests the origin or continuation of the hole may be tied to titan ambition and direct contact with [[Aeris]].

In session 031, [[Red Caesar]] and [[Ceril]] delivered [[Jack Harvey]]'s condensed [[Penumbra]] hoard to [[Lyngbakr]]. Lyngbakr consumed enough sky-material to repair the heavens, rising into the sky with Ceril and [[Alamar]] while black sky-matter sealed the central hole and smaller wounds across Stark. The sky closed behind them and became whole blue sky for the first time in Red Caesar's life.
