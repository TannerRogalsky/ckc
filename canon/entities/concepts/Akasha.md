A silvery-white liquid substance that falls from the sky as periodic rainstorms over [[Continental Stark]]. The DM connects it to the "oceans above" — the liquid said to come from [[Arcadia]] that fell through the [[Hole in the Sky]].

When the Akasha rains fall, they deal radiant damage to unprotected creatures and leave glowing, radiation-like burns. The fog that suffuses the jungle also carries Akasha energy. After the rain subsides, residual Akasha pools on the ground like quicksilver before soaking into the earth.

[[Vokunar]]'s sword contains a similar liquid, and the DM suggests the forge master who enchanted it may have collected Akasha from the mountains in the dwarven region. The substance may also be tied to the [[Penumbra]] and the broader cosmology of the fallen sky.
