A titan rite in which an aspirant climbs from the mountains high enough to physically touch the sky. It is not a select few who attempt it — anyone who thinks of doing it can try. It is not a process of good or evil with no moral value to it; it simply proves that the climber is the "damn best at everything" and that their ambition is maximized. Those who actually reach the top are the ones who count.

Since the sky is [[Aeris]]'s body, touching the sky means directly and physically touching the most powerful entity over Stark — nobody in [[Arcadia]] can claim that feat. The rite permits descent into Stark. [[Domyx]] completed the rite, and [[Sigil]] later revealed that [[Emperor Shen]] had done so before him and is the party's greatest enemy. His ancestor may have wanted to reach [[Arcadia]] itself, and was banished from his home afterward.
