Rare herbs that grow exclusively underwater in the [[Mana Sea]]. They are the correct cure for [[Mana Sickness]], the plague spreading through [[Southport]]. A human couple encountered by the party had mistakenly purchased surface herbs intended for a different magical illness, not knowing they needed deep roses.
