The main ship of [[The League of New Stark]]. Colors are white and cyan. Commanded by [[Lady Jacinthe]]. Crew includes [[Imotep]] and [[Damien Ouranous]].

In session 016, [[Red Caesar]] used Teleport Circle to bring the party to the ship's receiving room, where guards watched the permanent circle. Lady Jacinthe hosted the party and arranged their return to [[Lyngbakr Lagoon]] through [[Imotep]].
