The main ship of [[The League of New Stark]]. Colors are white and cyan. Commanded by [[Lady Jacinthe]]. Crew includes [[Imotep]] and [[Damien Ouranous]].

In session 016, [[Red Caesar]] used Teleport Circle to bring the party to the ship's receiving room, where guards watched the permanent circle. Lady Jacinthe hosted the party and arranged their return to [[Lyngbakr Lagoon]] through [[Imotep]].

In session 022, [[Red Caesar]] and [[Ceril]] arrived via Teleportation Circle. The ship had a transporter room with a docking point on the outside. The ship was carrying refugees from flooded regions. [[Damien Ouranous]] greeted the party and relayed that [[Lady Jacinthe]] was inconsolable over [[Obould]]'s fate. The party was given lodging in room 17. Ceril explored the ship's interior, noting heavy security around Jacinthe's quarters (guards at the staircase, trusted nobility at the doors), a laundry service, and a wine cellar with a frazzled half-orc sommelier.
