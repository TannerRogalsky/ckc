The imperial flagship of the [[Broyish Empire]] fleet that pursued [[The Opal]] and [[The Carrot Cake]] in session 031. [[Tango]] identified the ship by name while scouting from the air.

After [[The Opal]] escaped the first naval engagement by turning invisible, the Imperial Xihe reached [[Lyngbakr Lagoon]]. It sailed near the hidden [[The Opal|Opal]] without detecting it, then sent troops ashore as [[Emperor Shen]] and [[Vizier Jade]] confronted the party with [[Obould]] as hostage.
