A hot air balloon operated by the [[Broyish Empire]] as an aerial combat platform. Gun balloons bear imperial markings and carry mounted weapons including spike cannons and rotary guns. They can dispense imperial warriors from their gondolas onto enemy vessels below.

In session 026, three gun balloons attack [[The Opal]] while the party is returning from [[Xarag's Island]]. Imperial warriors in masks leap from the gondolas onto the ship's deck, triggering a major battle. The party defeats the boarders and destroys one balloon with fire. [[Red Caesar]] captures a second balloon by casting Otiluke's Resilient Sphere inside the cramped gondola, forcing the imperial crew out and causing the balloon to descend onto the ship. [[Vokenar]] charms the pilot of the third balloon into flying north and abandoning the fight.

After the battle, the party retains one gun balloon as a captured vessel. It is damaged but functional, giving the party the means for aerial travel across Stark. The balloon will require supplies and repairs at port.
