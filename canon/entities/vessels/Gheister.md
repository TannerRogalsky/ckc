A ship in [[Obould]]'s fleet, second to [[The Opal]]. It previously received visas from [[Beryzoz Helmscar]] in [[The Pit]].

In session 015, the party used the Gheister to chase the hobgoblins' second vessel after the boarding attack on [[The Opal]]. The party later took the Gheister as a longboat to reach [[Lyngbakr Lagoon]] and approach the risen [[Castle Kaedon]].
