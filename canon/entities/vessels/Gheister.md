A ship in [[Obould]]'s fleet, second to [[The Opal]]. It previously received visas from [[Beryzoz Helmscar]] in [[The Pit]].

In session 015, the party used the Gheister to chase the hobgoblins' second vessel after the boarding attack on [[The Opal]]. The party later took the Gheister as a longboat to reach [[Lyngbakr Lagoon]] and approach the risen [[Castle Kaedon]].

In session 024, [[Theo Harvey]] stole the Gheister during his escape from [[The Opal]]. [[Kerben]] had secretly freed Theo and arranged for him to board the Gheister under cover of fog and darkness. Theo departed without revealing his destination, though he hinted he would lay low — possibly near the [[Harengon Warrens]]. The party did not know where he was heading.
