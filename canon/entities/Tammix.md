A dwarf fighter from the [[Steelfend Clan]] and brother of [[Gammix]]. Initially fought against the party under [[Morel Chainsunder]]'s magical control. After being freed, he joined the party and used a heavy crossbow in combat. He knows the layout of the [[Darvinblast]] and led the party to his clan's hidden house. Chose to stay with his clan after the battles concluded.
