A rogue with a sailor background who speaks Northern (dwarvish). Travels aboard [[The Opal]] and works with [[Raxxy]] on navigation and astrology duties. Travels with [[Zulu]], a dinosaur companion that can be given grasshopper legs via a jump scroll. Has poison resistance, likely from childhood exposure to toxic fumes in the dwarven deep world. Uses Hunter's Mark, Ensnaring Strike, dual-wields scimitars, and carries a musket. Has an arcane keyring with charges for Zone of Truth and Arcane Lock. Knows knots and can make restraints. Can activate Stone Cutting and Tremor Sense abilities. Effectively uses a ranger/rogue multiclass build optimized for spiking down individual targets with Hunter's Mark, sneak attack, and scimitar melee. Fights alongside [[Gammix]] and [[Tammix]] during the atrium battle. Sent his companion [[Zulu]] to charge and finish off enemies. Offers the enemy the same ultimatum given to previous dwarves: join the party or face consequences.
