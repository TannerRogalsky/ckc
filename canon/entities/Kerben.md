A rogue with a sailor background who speaks Northern (dwarvish). Travels aboard [[The Opal]] and works with [[Raxxy]] on navigation and astrology duties. Travels with [[Zulu]], a dinosaur companion that can be given grasshopper legs via a jump scroll. Has poison resistance, likely from childhood exposure to toxic fumes in the dwarven deep world. Uses Hunter's Mark, Ensnaring Strike, dual-wields scimitars, and carries a musket. Has an arcane keyring with charges for Zone of Truth and Arcane Lock. Knows knots and can make restraints. Can activate Stone Cutting and Tremor Sense abilities. Effectively uses a ranger/rogue multiclass build optimized for spiking down individual targets with Hunter's Mark, sneak attack, and scimitar melee. Fights alongside [[Gammix]] and [[Tammix]] during the atrium battle. Sent his companion [[Zulu]] to charge and finish off enemies. Offers the enemy the same ultimatum given to previous dwarves: join the party or face consequences. In [[Darvinblast]], used Stone Cunning to map the underground terrain and identify paths. Switched from musket to dual scimitars in melee against the [[Deep World Wizard]], dealing 33 damage with sneak attack and [[Zulu]]'s charge. Has a preternatural dwarf sense for gems and precious minerals — found a rare ruby embedded in a newly-mined wall section. In a second ambush in [[Darvinblast]], demonstrated exceptional sniping capability: with Hunter's Mark and sneak attack combined, obliterated a Deep World priest for 38 damage in a single volley. Later repositioned to focus fire on the second [[Deep World Wizard]], switching Hunter's Mark targets as needed. Zulu (his dinosaur companion) was kept ready to charge down the hallway.

In the triceratops encounter, cast Speak with Animals to communicate with the mount before battle. Used the commands to direct the triceratops in combat, commanding it to charge and gore the [[Fire Giant]], knocking it down and pushing it off a 60-foot bridge. Shot the falling giant with Ensnaring Strike (level 2). Jumped Zulu into the pit (17 fall damage) to join the fight. During the confrontation with [[Morel Chainsunder]] in the church, Hunter's Marked Morel and shot him repeatedly, revealing his true face — a dwarf with tentacle-like painted hair — demonstrating to the congregation that he was mortal and bleedable. Found a posting in Northern with triceratops commands after Speak with Animals wore off. Found texts on the deep worlders during looting. Received Deep World ivory figures as treasure.

**Level 8:** Advanced to Rogue 3, choosing Arcane Trickster subclass. Gains access to wizard spells (three prepared from spellbook) and bonus action stealth. Considering Assassin rogue archetype for initiative advantage and first-round sneak attack bonuses (+3 damage).
