A powerful vampire lord that occupied the front car of [[Hole on Wheels]] in [[The Carrot Cake]].

The vampiric nightbringer had a faceless head with a screaming mouth and broad white angelic wings. It commanded vampiric spawn, familiars, and other creatures throughout the train. When wounded, it shed pale feathers that later turned black and drifted away.

The creature could drain vitality, cast oppressive magic, and regenerate unless exposed to sunlight. The party defeated it by opening the train to the sun: [[Red Caesar]] blew out the front window, [[Ceril]] tore open the roof with lightning, [[Vokenar]] broke its domination spell, and [[Domyx]] finally hurled it from the moving train. It disintegrated into blackening feathers after its defeat.
