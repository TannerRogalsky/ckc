Gigantic turtle/whale things whose main trait is that people are surprised they aren't islands. They have a magical aura that makes people perceive them as islands. Generally benign — sort of hungry and instinctual rather than curious. [[Lyngbakr]] is a member of this species. A famous Saratan named [[Wyrm]] is noted in records. [[Aeris]] may know something about this species that isn't in the books.
