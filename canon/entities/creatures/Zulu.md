A dinosaur companion belonging to [[Kerben]]. Can be enhanced with grasshopper legs via a jump scroll, granting increased mobility. Has a charged attack that adds an extra die of damage. Navigates cramped dwarven corridors well.

Zulu was knocked down by a [[Nalfeshne]]'s Fireball in [[Castle Kaedon]], and [[Kerben]] used magic to restore him. In a later encounter, Zulu was struck again by the Nalfeshne's Lightning Bolt and knocked down before being restored by the party's healing.
