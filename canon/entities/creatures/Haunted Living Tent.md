A gargantuan living tent encountered at [[The Carrot Cake]] midway. It siphons victims into an extra-dimensional interior space: a beautiful tea cozy house with furniture, bay windows, and a green field visible outside. Inside, a [[Magen]] sits drinking tea, and the tent's furniture becomes animated poltergeist weapons that slam occupants against walls.

The tent is fixed in place, and its interior blocks ordinary escape magic. The windows are painted illusions, and attacking the walls from within reveals darkness beyond. The party destroyed it through attacks from both inside and outside, causing it to deflate and disintegrate into starry particles.
