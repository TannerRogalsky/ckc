An owl familiar belonging to [[Ceril]]. Named "Nyquil." Can communicate telepathically with Ceril within 100 feet, conveying information through metaphor and faint images. Capable of long-distance scouting — followed an imperial flying scout for over 25 miles before returning.

Killed by [[Akasha]] rain on [[Continental Stark]] during session 014. The owl suffered radiant burns and a radioactive glow from the toxic rain. [[Ceril]] consumed the owl's body whole.
