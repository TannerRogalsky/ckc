A two-headed giant creature encountered in [[Darvinblast]]. Each head has a pair of tusks and a grayish-black beard, and the heads can communicate with each other during combat. Wields a battle axe in one hand and a morning star in the other, treating both as two-handed weapons. The morning star can knock targets prone and impose disadvantage on their next attack. Having two heads grants immunity to certain effects like strangulation. Was brought into the fight by a [[Deep World Wizard]] who lives in a large house within the city.
