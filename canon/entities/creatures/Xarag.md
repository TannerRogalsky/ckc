Black dragons are an ongoing threat tied to [[Castle Kaedon]]. The party first encountered a larger black dragon in the prologue; it wrecked the hold of [[The Opal]] and stole their gold. The party felt they were not yet strong enough to fight it, though it remained a prologue quest they could revisit.

In session 018, an adult black dragon burst from the ocean waters around [[Castle Kaedon]] alongside four black dragon wyrmlings from the same clutch. [[Ceril]] recognized them as offspring of the dragon the party had seen earlier. The wyrmlings had spread across the terrain since [[The Cataclysm]], and this clutch's ability to swim through the ocean as easily as fly gave them an advantage. The adult dragon declared that "father will be pleased" when it found the castle, hinting that the larger prologue dragon remains active. The party engaged the clutch in aerial combat, with [[Vokunar]]'s Sacred Spear striking all five dragons and [[Ceril]]'s Wall of Fire trapping the adult.

The battle concluded with [[Domyx]] delivering the final blow to the adult dragon after [[Vokunar]] restored [[Ceril]] to consciousness. During the fight, [[Kerben]] triggered a hidden mangonel trap before its enchantment faded. After the battle, Kerben harvested the dragon's resilient acid-resistant scales and crafted [[Preparation Melf]] — a throwable acid flask weapon.

In session 026, the party confronts the larger prologue black dragon on [[Xarag's Island]]. The dragon's name is revealed to be **Xarag**. The island is surrounded by luminescent algal bloom and mist, with dark terrain and glowing cyan tide pools. The dragon is resting but awake, and black dragon eggs are nearby. The party tracks the dragon using visual tracking and [[Red Caesar]]'s Locate Creature spell. The party drinks potions of acid resistance before engaging. [[Kerben]] opens with a devastating critical hit through the dragon's mouth from long range. The dragon uses acid breath and legendary actions, summoning black gadflies that poison Kerben. [[Vokunar]] summons a Spiritual Weapon in the form of [[Crone]]'s bastard sword.

The battle concludes with [[Kerben]] delivering the killing shots. Xarag writhes and finally dies, splashing into the algal bloom. After the dragon falls, [[Kerben]] salvages the corpse, using dragon scales and acid gizzard to craft two permanent potions of acid resistance that persist even after Tasha's Cauldron is reused. The party then searches the island and recovers their lost treasure from a hollow in the cliff face: 200 pounds of gold pieces, five units of mithril, and bags of gems that once belonged to [[The Opal]] and [[Obould]]. The mithril likely came from another ship — possibly an elven vessel — that also met disaster near the island. Among the recovered items are the [[Orkland Pin of Courage]] and [[Obould's Wedding Band]], both deeply personal items of Obould's.
