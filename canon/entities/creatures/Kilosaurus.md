The triceratops-like beast befriended in [[Darvinblast]] and named by [[Kerben]]. Cow-like in appearance, [[Kerben]] named it [[Kilosaurus]] ("Kilo" meaning heavy, phonetically similar to "Cow"). It is stronger than an elephant and capable of towing several tons when given wheels or proper preparation.

In session 013, [[The Opal]] was reinforced partly to carry this creature, and it helped haul a purified [[Penumbra]] core back to the ship. The party later left it at [[Lyngbakr Lagoon]] to help haul stone and defend the developing base.
