A gorgon head is a monstrosity — the floating head of a woman wreathed in snakes, with no body below the neck, only a fusion of serpentine material trailing behind it. Encountered in the water features of the [[Magic Hat]] area of [[The Carrot Cake]], gorgon heads attack with poison rays, snake hair strikes that deal poison damage, and a petrifying gaze that turns targets to stone. When killed, the head bursts and releases a wave of black-and-white energy that forces nearby creatures to make constitution saves or become restrained and potentially petrified. Gorgon heads appeared alongside [[Flame Skull]]s as enemies guarding the [[Magic Hat]] area and as wave enemies at [[King of the Hole]].
