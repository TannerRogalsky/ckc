A large, bulky creature with a single eye in the middle of its body. It serves as a companion to [[Vizier Jade]] and was sent by her to search for multiple locations, including [[The Carrot Cake]]. The Tyrant never returned from that mission.

## Plot Events

In session 023, [[Theo Harvey]] revealed to [[Domyx]] that [[Vizier Jade]] had sent the Tyrant to find [[The Carrot Cake]]. The Tyrant was never seen again, presumably because the Carrot Cake's maze of illusions and arcane traps captured or destroyed it. Theo noted that if one wound up inside the Carrot Cake, they would not return either.

In session 029, the DM clarified that the Tyrant was likely sent to search for more than one location, not just the Carrot Cake. Vizier Jade may have had suspicions about the area being higher than normal, and the Tyrant's failure to return could have been due to the Carrot Cake's timed opening window rather than the creature being destroyed.
