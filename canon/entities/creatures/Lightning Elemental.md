An elemental creature composed of crackling electricity, encountered as a wave enemy in [[King of the Hole]] within [[The Carrot Cake]]. The lightning elemental leaps into the air by pushing like a spring from the ground and attacks with electrical arcs that shock targets. It is notably fast and mobile, able to reposition quickly across the battlefield.

The lightning elemental appeared alongside a [[Shambling Mound]] in the King of the Hole's wave combat. Notably, its lightning attacks healed the shambling mound rather than damaging it — the lightning arcing into the mound of vines restored its vitality instead of harming it, creating a dangerous synergy between the two enemies.
