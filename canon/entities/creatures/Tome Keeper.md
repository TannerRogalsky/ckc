An [[Arcanoloth]] who keeps the [[Tome of Satariel]] wherever he hides. He commands a portion of the ocean and could be the most powerful outsider in that region. The [[Rakshasa]] revealed that the Tome Keeper can see him wherever he travels and could summon other escaped demons to find and kill him as one. If the party finds and kills the Tome Keeper, it would give the Rakshasa an opportunity to leave his hiding place and visit his family. The Tome Keeper cannot be reasoned with — demons only understand power.

## Session 023

[[Ceril]]'s Commune with Nature revealed that the Tome Keeper is an [[Arcanoloth]] who has built [[Tome Keeper's Pyramid]] — a temple in the eastern waters roughly 10 miles from the party's position. The temple was constructed approximately 10 to 25 years ago and serves as a tabernacle whose sole purpose is to contain the [[Tome of Satariel]]. Dragonflies appeared as indicators that the fiend was within three miles.

[[Vokunar]]'s Legend Lore confirmed that the Tome Keeper can only capture knowledge through death — he had to kill and extract the souls of 666 wizards to create the Tome's pages. The temple's interior mirrors its state in hell and contains traps and treacheries set by the Tome Keeper. The Tome Keeper will try to capture the souls of magical beings who enter his temple; the Tome likely has more than 666 pages and souls within it, as lesser souls eventually stopped being sufficient and he seeks demon souls which are worth more in hell.

The party chose not to attack the pyramid immediately, judging themselves unprepared. They discussed several countermeasures: Magic Jar to remove their souls from their bodies before engaging (though this leaves the body catatonic), Protection from Good and Evil for partial resistance against soul capture, and [[Red Caesar]]'s abjuration spells for additional protection. All three casters — [[Vokunar]], [[Red Caesar]], and [[Ceril]] — have access to Planar Binding, which they considered using to either force the Tome Keeper into their service or as insurance during a confrontation. [[Vokunar]] can also cast Banishment as a backup. They planned to scout the pyramid with familiars and divination spells before committing, and intended to stock up on scrolls in [[Broy]] or at [[Cutlass Cray]] before returning.
