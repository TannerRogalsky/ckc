An [[Arcanoloth]] who keeps the [[Tome of Satariel]] wherever he hides. He commands a portion of the ocean and could be the most powerful outsider in that region. The [[Rakshasa]] revealed that the Tome Keeper can see him wherever he travels and could summon other escaped demons to find and kill him as one. If the party finds and kills the Tome Keeper, it would give the Rakshasa an opportunity to leave his hiding place and visit his family. The Tome Keeper cannot be reasoned with — demons only understand power.
