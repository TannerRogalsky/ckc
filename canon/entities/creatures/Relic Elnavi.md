A blink dog that came with the party after their adventure at [[Otyugh Isle]].

Relic Elnavi serves as the ship's janitor aboard [[The Opal]], using its blink ability to teleport trash and misplaced items to where they belong — making the tiny repetitive tasks nearly invisible to the crew. After sensing someone living at [[Lyngbakr Lagoon]], it directed [[Domyx]]'s attention to a bonfire there. It chose to stay at the lagoon with [[Alamar]] after sensing [[Ceril]]'s anxiety about returning to the elven lands. It had once lived among high elves and offered [[Ceril]] a small hope that the elven world after [[The Cataclysm]] might not be worse than the old one.
