A swarm of severed hands that crawl like spiders on their fingertips and thumb, using five finger-attachments as legs. The hands are blackened and scuttle through dense grass and seaweed in the [[Castle Kaedon Arena]] exterior grounds.

They appear to be carried by the wind and fog from the cataract (giant waterfall) and seem to coordinate, making hand gestures before targeting specific opponents. Area effects can scatter and thin the swarm.
