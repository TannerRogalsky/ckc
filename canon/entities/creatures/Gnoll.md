Fiendish hyena-like people who survived [[The Cataclysm]] by racing inland, killing competing inhabitants, and rapidly claiming highlands and hills. They have intelligence in the 6–12 range, fight in packs, and carry natural poison. Their creature type is fiend (not humanoid). In session 013, packs of gnolls guarded a pure [[Penumbra]] chunk in northern territory near [[Brimbolyn]]. They favor pack tactics, bone weapons (bone bows, bone whips, bone flails), and poison. Encountered variants include Gnoll Fangs, Gnoll Warriors, Gnoll Thanes, and Gnoll Pack Lords (who can use Incite Rampage to direct allies).

In the battle's conclusion, the party eliminated all gnoll leaders first, leaving only stragglers. [[Vokenar]]'s Wall of Fire proved effective at controlling movement — gnolls had to leap through it to reach the party, and projectiles from beyond the wall burned up. [[Domyx]] crit-punched a pack lord to death. [[Red Caesar]] used Magic Missile to split two weakened targets in a double snipe. [[Kerben]] overkilled a remaining gnoll so severely its blood dispersed 40 feet. After the fight, [[Red Caesar]]'s Locate Creature confirmed no gnolls remained within 1000 feet in any direction.
