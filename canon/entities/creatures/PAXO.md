A warforged construct found in the [[Southern Archipelago Castle]]. About human-sized, made of metal pieces with a cloak and a smooth, reflective faceplate made of an unfamiliar alloy of quicksilver, gold, and silver that could change shape and produce illusion-like effects through electricity rather than magic. It introduced itself as "PAXO" and spoke of eradicating lifeforms. It wielded heat beams, could pass through walls, and had legendary actions including a supernatural slow effect emitted from its faceplate. It was resistant to fire. PAXO was the "wicked, twisted" construct that drove [[Witness]] and the castle's human inhabitants to flee. The party defeated it in close-quarters combat; [[Kerben]]'s critical strike with the [[Shark's Edge]] popped the faceplate clean off, and [[Red Caesar]]'s Bigby's Hand crushed the body into a twisted tin can. The faceplate was recovered as a potential treasure.
