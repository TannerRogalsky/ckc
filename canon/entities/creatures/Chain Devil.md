A devil composed entirely of chains, with pits in its face where eyes should be. Chain devils use their chains to pull enemies closer and string people up; they are considered slave masters of hell. They possess an unnerving gaze that fires beams of red light from their eye-pits, inflicting supernatural fear on targets.

Chain devils are lower on the fiendish hierarchy and favor physical attributes over mental ones. They have magic resistance like other fiends.

One was encountered in [[Castle Kaedon]] alongside a [[Bearded Devil]], summoned through magic circles. Notably, the chain devil and bearded devil were cooperating despite normally being enemies in the blood war between demons and devils. A [[Nalfeshne]] was observed buffing the chain devil with temporary hit points before departing.
