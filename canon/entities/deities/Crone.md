Also known as the Moon. One of three sister goddesses alongside [[Aeris]] and [[Sigil]]. Her domain is Time, Death, and Passage of Things. The phase of the moon matters when contacting her. Speaks to [[Vokunar]] during his training in the place outside of time. Works alongside Sigil to prepare Vokunar as a weapon. More measured than her sisters, advocating for giving Vokunar time to understand what he has learned. Refined the elemental constructs Vokunar fights during training. Appears as a crescent moon image on ceilings when [[Ceril]] casts Moonbeam. A single person (the Crone) caused [[The Cataclysm]] and achieved what they desired with it.

In session 015, [[Vokunar]] summoned Crone during a full moon at [[The Garden]]. She appeared clad in chainmail, dragging a blood-soaked greatsword, exhausted from fighting. The room's walls peeled away to reveal a shimmering silver moon; to everyone else, the bed began bleeding. She confirmed there is time to stop the [[Demi-Spell]] but warned the party is more likely to die than run out of time. She revealed that [[Aeris]] (the Sky) is injured but could heal on her own if given enough power — the party doesn't need all the [[Penumbra]], just enough to restore Aeris's strength so she can heal the rest. She advised using the [[Rakshasa]] as a bargaining chip: demons only understand violence, so the party could prove power to the [[Tome Keeper]] and offer it its life in exchange for leading them to the Rakshasa. When asked if the elves would stop the Demi-Spell if the sky were repaired, she answered no — the elves crave witnessing the [[Genesis Mundi]] and will not change their minds.
