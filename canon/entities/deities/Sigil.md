Also known as the Sun. One of three sister goddesses alongside [[Aeris]] and [[Crone]]. Her domain is Life, Energy, and Motion (Sky/Life/Energy/Motion — grounded in the present). Speaks to [[Vokunar]] during his training. More aggressive in approach than Crone, pushing Vokunar harder and faster. Wants Vokunar to slumber less and train more. Told the party that their mission is not hopeless and that they will only have to give [[Penumbra]] to [[Lyngbakr]] the turtle. Gave [[Ceril]] a shield.
