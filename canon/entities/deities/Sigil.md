Also known as the Sun. One of three sister goddesses alongside [[Aeris]] and [[Crone]]. Her domain is Life, Energy, and Motion — grounded in the present. She is good at making decisions that keep people safe and healthy. She possesses constant Locate Creature on every creature currently active in Stark, effectively maintaining a living radar of all beings.

Speaks to [[Vokunar]] during his training. More aggressive in approach than Crone, pushing Vokunar harder and faster. Wants Vokunar to slumber less and train more. Told the party that their mission is not hopeless and that they will only have to give [[Penumbra]] to [[Lyngbakr]] the turtle. Gave [[Ceril]] a shield.

When [[Vokunar]] contacts her, the colored gems in his mantle turn into white diamonds and a brilliant sun rises from them for his eyes only. Only [[Vokunar]] can hear her answers — he must relay them to the party.

When contacted in session 013, [[Sigil]] advised the party to head first to the elves by the shortest wind-favored route, revealed that [[The Order of Seasons]] had moved [[Penumbra]] beyond the jungles, confirmed [[Alamar]] is an ally but will withhold truths, said allies will appear for [[Lyngbakr Lagoon]], and named [[Domyx's Ancestor]] as the party's greatest enemy.
