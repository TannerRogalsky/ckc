A legendary pirate and father of [[Xander MacLenth]]. Known as "Lenth the Rugged," he reportedly had a wife on every continent — one wife of each race, plus some of elemental varieties. He "sowed his wild oats across the land" and wanted to leave a lasting legacy, unlike his son who was "a pirate through and through" wanting only to spend his wealth before his watery grave. His legend was unknown to the party, and the DM offered it as a potential future quest line which the players declined.
