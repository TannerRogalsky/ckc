A party member who favors kicking doors open and fighting with rage. Uses a halberd and has divine smite (Holy Light). Served as obstacle-course navigator in an earlier dungeon. Known for bold intimidation tactics, including grappling dwarf fighters and hurling them over garbage ravines. Has Cloud Jaunt (10-foot range), teleport javelin, magnetic boots, and Stoic Laurels. Travels aboard [[The Opal]]. Has a base movement speed of 67 feet, the highest on the team. Uses Recklessness and Divine Fury (choosing necrotic damage) with his halberd. Critically struck a [[Bat Rider]] with an uppercut move triggering Divine Fury. Uses his high mobility to position himself inside [[Ceril]]'s Moonbeam to hold enemies in place. Took heavy damage (17 points) from multiple grunts pouncing on him but survived through rage. Recruited [[Otto]] to join the crew alongside [[Vokunar]]. In combat with the [[Etten]], was knocked prone and took significant damage from its morning star (which imposes disadvantage on the next attack). Used Sentinel to make opportunity attacks against the [[Deep World Wizard]]. Finished off the Deep World Wizard with a melee slash. Took 24 force damage from the wizard's grab attack (reduced to 20 with temp HP from [[Vokunar]]). Took 8 fire damage from Hellish Rebuke on the wizard's death. Received a [[Potion of Proof Against Storms]] from a mysterious dwarf in the walls who questioned [[Morel Chainsunder]]'s plans. In a second ambush deeper in [[Darvinblast]], was targeted by a [[Deep World Wizard]]'s Blight spell (level 4) for 36 necrotic damage — described as feeling like rapid aging with dust coming off his body. Used reroll-on-fail rage ability for the save. Cleared out grunts efficiently, finishing one with two attacks. Later returned to finish off the second Deep World Wizard, who cast Hellish Rebuke as a dying reaction (Domyx took only 6 fire damage with advantage on dex save).

In the triceratops encounter, activated rage and sprinted 134 feet forward to engage. Finished the commandant, leaving only the uncommanded triceratops. During the [[Fire Giant]] fight, activated rage and dodged while the party closed distance. Used Enlarge (bonus action, once per long rest) and leaped 60 feet into the pit, landing on the giant with an elbow drop grapple. Took 21+21 fire damage from the giant's flame sword attacks while grappling it in the burning miasma below. The Fire Giant recognized Domyx as "one giant to another" and told him to "return to the sky." Recklessly attacked with trident using necrotic smite. Vokunar's Warding Flare kept him above zero. Climbed out of the pit while shrinking back to normal size. Used a potion (d20+4) for 12 HP during the short rest afterward.

During the church confrontation, took 51 fire damage from [[Morel Chainsunder]]'s Flamestrike — devastated, running on his last hit points. Showed his sky-blue hands (imbued from touching the sky) to the congregation as proof of the surface world, giving a powerful speech about self-determination and freedom while barely alive. Removed cowls from congregants to show them as individuals.

**Level 8:** Took ability score improvement, raising Constitution from 14 to 16 (+8 max HP, hit dice modifier improved).
