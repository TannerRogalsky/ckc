An aasimar born in [[Brimbalin]] 45 years ago to an aasimar mother and half-elf father. His mother fell from [[Arcadia]] and met his father below. His father took ill and they left; Osiris suspects they may have gone to Southport.

Osiris believes the Magic City and [[The Order of Seasons]] are "one and the same." He told [[Vokunar]] that the Order is dedicated to achieving the [[Demi-Spell]] — a grand reassembly that will rewrite the past to undo the damage of [[The Cataclysm]]. He warned that the Demi-Spell may be less than a year away and that those who oppose the Order never turn out right. He mentioned that decades ago, someone tried to stop the Demi-Spell and destroy all who knew it — possibly [[Morel Chainsunder]].
