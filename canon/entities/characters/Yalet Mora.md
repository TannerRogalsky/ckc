A stone-bodied crew member aboard [[The Opal]] who can roll around in boulder form. He suggested that the crew recruit a wind-aligned creature to help power the sails when winds fail.

He is the brother of [[Yalet Aurum]], a Galeb Duurr with a body of solid gold encased in a monument outside [[Brimbalin]]. Yalet Aurum claims superiority over Yalet Mora, saying Yalet Mora "produces only small rocks" while Yalet Aurum "produces only mountains."
