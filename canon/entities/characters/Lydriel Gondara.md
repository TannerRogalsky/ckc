A half-elf member of [[The Order of Seasons]] who works alongside [[Keys]] at [[The Academy]]. She was previously encountered by [[Ceril]] at [[Castle Kaedon]], where she and Keys stole a piece of [[Penumbra]]. She recognized Ceril when they met at the Academy.

She leads the party's discussion about the [[Demi-Spell]] and its moral implications, arguing that fixing the sky is "another way of depriving people of choice." She negotiated the accord with the party to stop collecting [[Penumbra]] temporarily.
