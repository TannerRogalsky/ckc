A blood relative of [[Domyx]] from [[Clan Akathia]] who touched the sky before [[Domyx]] and was erased from titan records. He was banished from his home after [[Touching the Sky]].

[[Sigil]] identified this ancestor as the party's greatest enemy, perhaps their only great enemy. Since [[Touching the Sky]] means physically touching [[Aeris]] herself, this ancestor directly contacted the most powerful entity over Stark.

In session 023, this ancestor was revealed to be [[Emperor Shen]] of the [[Broyish Empire]]. Shen confirmed he was the first of his bloodline to touch the sky and that Domyx would be the last. He tried to destroy the sky using [[Starfall]] and was not entirely successful. His goal is to kill the gods, believing they play with mortals like toys.
