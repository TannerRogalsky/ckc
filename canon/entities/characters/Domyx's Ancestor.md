An unidentified blood relative of [[Domyx]] from [[Clan Akathia]] who touched the sky before [[Domyx]] and was erased from titan records. He was banished from his home after [[Touching the Sky]]. He may have wanted to reach [[Arcadia]] itself, and may have thought of something so ambitious that it risked all life — though [[Domyx]] is unsure whether his ancestor was aware of the consequences.

[[Sigil]] identified this ancestor as the party's greatest enemy, perhaps their only great enemy. Since [[Touching the Sky]] means physically touching [[Aeris]] herself, this ancestor directly contacted the most powerful entity over Stark.
