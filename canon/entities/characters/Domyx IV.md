Domyx IV is the current ruler of [[Clan Akathia]] and father of [[Domyx]] and [[Lorelei]]. He rules the clan with strict enforcement of titan isolationist laws and has kept the truth about [[Emperor Shen]] hidden from his children and the broader clan.

## Plot Events

In session 024, Domyx IV confronted his returned son [[Domyx]] at the exterior of [[Akathia Manor]]. Initially showing a mix of genuine relief and anger, he attempted to negotiate a quiet resolution — offering to cover up Domyx's return and send him back to Stark. When Domyx refused to forget the truth about their grandfather and renounced his family name by discarding his Akathian medallion, Domyx IV disowned him, declaring "You are an Akathian no more. You are my son no more." He departed in sorrow, ending their relationship.
