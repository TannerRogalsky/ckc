Leader of [[The Garden]], a druid sanctuary. The Garden is building a fleet of boats with cyan and green banners bearing a tree, extending friendship and aid to human civilizations and settlements. Druids raise crops, bring livestock, and supply food to those in need — though the growing threat of creatures on the waters may soon require them to ask [[The League of New Stark]] for protection.

In session 015, [[Vokunar]] used Locate Object to find a drained [[Penumbra]] chunk in Theotropa's possession. She agreed to hand over the Penumbra if the party destroyed a [[Storm Phoenix]] — a being of pure elemental power driven mad by [[The Cataclysm]] — located south of the Garden. When killed, the phoenix reverts to its egg form and is reborn naturally.

When the party returned after destroying the Storm Phoenix, Theotropa confirmed the task was complete — lightning had struck deep into one of her woodlands and storms had been returned to the region. She explained that lightning brings destruction but new life grows from the ashes, much like the phoenix itself. She gave the party the drained Penumbra and noted that someone had come looking for it in their absence — a scout from the [[Broyish Empire]] who had appeared as a cloud on some device. The Penumbra was under non-detection, so the scout left empty-handed. Theotropa offered to hold onto any future Penumbra the party found, since others were coming for it.

[[Alamar]] later revealed that a druid garden existed in his time as well, led by a kindly woman named Theotropa — the same name as the current leader. This suggests Theotropa may persist across ages or that the name carries deep significance within the druid tradition.
