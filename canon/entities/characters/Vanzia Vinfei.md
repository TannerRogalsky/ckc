A high elf paladin and revolutionary who escaped the elven lands with [[Ceril]] after [[Queen Caeradwyn]]'s death. She felt she had shirked her duty by leaving other captives behind, but followed [[Ceril]] west after he saw lines of light crossing the sky in that direction.
