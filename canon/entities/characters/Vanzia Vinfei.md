A high elf paladin and revolutionary who escaped the elven lands with [[Ceril]] after [[Queen Caeradwyn]]'s death. She felt she had shirked her duty by leaving other captives behind, but followed [[Ceril]] west after he saw lines of light crossing the sky in that direction.

Decades later, Vanzia had thrown her lot in with the [[Broyish Empire]], believing it would prevent elves from rising again in the form they had been under [[Queen Caeradwyn]]. She told Ceril she had found a place in the empire where she could start fresh and where that queen could never come to power again.

In session 023, she was encountered in the [[Broyish Capital]] working alongside [[Naomi]] — the underground fixer who absolves criminal records. Vanzia was present when the party met Naomi and recognized Ceril after decades apart. She teased Ceril about their old friendship and humorously claimed an oath of "honesty above all else" while operating in a criminal den. She confirmed that [[Obould]] is in the custody of [[Vizier Jade]] as prisoner of war and leverage against [[The League of New Stark]]. She offered to lead the party to Obould and arrange visitor papers, but warned that Jade would need to be confronted first.
