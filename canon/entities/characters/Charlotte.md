Sister of [[Ulrich Fjöler]] and sister-in-law to [[Lorelei]]. She works at [[Akathia Manor]] preparing food and performing general maintenance duties. She was present when [[Domyx]] confronted his family about the truth of [[Domyx's Ancestor]]'s crimes.

## Plot Events

In session 024, Charlotte approached [[Domyx]] upon his arrival at [[Akathia Manor]] and warned him that his return was against clan rules. When Domyx revealed the truth about his grandfather breaking the sky, Charlotte was shocked. She overheard [[Lorelei]] emerge from hiding, confirming that Lorelei had heard everything about their grandfather. Charlotte backed away from the confrontation as Lorelei revealed her pregnancy and plan to flee with Ulrich.
