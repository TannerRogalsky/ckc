A divine being known as the Rakshasa. He is [[Ceril]]'s father, whom Ceril visited in a cave for years. Ceril rejected godhood and the path his father represented.

He was encountered at [[The Garden]] (the druid sanctuary) where he was guarded, though the party's earlier visit there did not result in a notable encounter with him. [[Keys]] and [[Lydriel Gondara]] were unaware of his existence.
