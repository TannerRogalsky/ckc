A dwarf fighter from the [[Steelfend Clan]] who initially fought against the party under [[Morel Chainsunder]]'s magical compulsion. After [[Red Caesar]] dispelled the control over his clan, Gammix joined the party alongside [[Tammix]]. He wields a heavy crossbow and fights alongside his brother. After the final battle in the [[Darvinblast]], he chose to remain with his family to help ensure their safety.
