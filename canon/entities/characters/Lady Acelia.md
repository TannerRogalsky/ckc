The younger sister of [[Alamar]] and youngest daughter of the Rodgard family. She was the last child born before their father [[King Maniasis]] succumbed to madness. After the crown was abdicated and the royal family scattered, Acelia reportedly traveled to the northern areas and began peace efforts with the dwarves. A Rodgard cousin was appointed to the Senate of [[Kaedon]]'s new government, though Acelia herself was not the senator. She likely perished in [[The Cataclysm]] along with most of the nobility.

Her presence in [[Castle Kaedon]] is commemorated by an oak chalice set with jade insets, carved with her name in a child's handwriting. The chalice was found in a guest room of the castle.
