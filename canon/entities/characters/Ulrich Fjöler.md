A close friend and loyalist of [[Clan Akathia]] who risked exile or execution by following [[Domyx]] down from the titan mountains. He warned [[Domyx]] that another blood relative from [[Clan Akathia]] had touched the sky before him, had been erased from the records, and still existed somewhere in Stark.

## Plot Events

In session 016, [[Lorelei]] revealed in a dream to [[Domyx]] that Ulrich had been imprisoned in dungeons for months after returning from seeking Domyx on the hill. He refuses to say why he went, but Lorelei believed it was to speak with Domyx about something deeply tied to clan ancestry and bloodline secrets. The clan has sentenced Ulrich to execution, and Lorelei is pregnant with his child — a fact that will soon become impossible to hide. Domyx pledged to rescue him.

In session 024, [[Charlotte]] revealed that Ulrich had told her before being taken away that what he discovered about the clan would bring it crumbling down — that [[Domyx's Ancestor]] was "some kind of a monster" but Ulrich never had time to research what specifically. Ulrich is being held in the [[Prison of Frost]], not far from [[Akathia Manor]]. [[Lorelei]] confirmed this location and indicated that [[Domyx]] would know how to reach it.

In session 024, the party reached the [[Prison of Frost]], a facility sealed behind a plane of pure magical energy — an abomination to titan folk who traditionally shun magic. [[Domyx]] presented himself without his Akathian title as a mark of his renounced identity. [[Ceril]] dispelled the barrier with ease, freeing Ulrich. He greeted Domyx and [[Lorelei]] with relief and asked if the world below was safe for them. With the barrier broken, Ulrich and Lorelei joined the party and returned to [[The Opal]] as new crew members.
