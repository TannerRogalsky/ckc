A young being trapped in stone for decades, falling through the sky toward [[Stark]]. 60 years ago, a young Vokunar stared out over the water of a citadel in Arkadia, where he saw [[Solar Flare]], a very tall woman with shining light. [[Gossa]], an aasimar mentor, suggested there are other paths than to become a Guardian and said "May Sigil's light guide you." Trained in a place outside of time and space by [[Crone]] and [[Sigil]], who prepare him as a weapon for an inevitable war. Has no memory of prior training sessions, each awakening feeling brand new. Can cast Create Water, Gust of Wind, Calm Emotions, Aura of Vitality, Warding Flare, Cure Wounds, and Nimbus Form. Has a sword he practices with in dreams. The goddesses estimate he has a few more decades, possibly years, before his descent ends. Uses a healing-focused combat style, repeatedly reviving defeated dwarf fighters to demonstrate goodwill and turn them from hostility to indifference. Cast Aura of Vitality (level 3) during the atrium battle, healing [[Red Caesar]], [[Kerben]], and [[Ceril]] extensively. Uses Toll the Dead (2d8) and Word of Radiance. Strategically uses Warding Flare to protect allies, prioritizing others over himself. Took bat bites willingly to save resources for protecting the party. Recruited [[Otto]] to join the crew alongside [[Domyx]]. In [[Darvinblast]], cast Water Walk ritual and took max HP drain from the toxic mist. Used Toll the Dead against the [[Etten]] for 11 damage. Cast Aura of Vitality (level 3) to heal the party after the fight, distributing healing among [[Domyx]], [[Ceril]], and [[Kerben]]. Has a sword that "doesn't work" the way he expects in this world — it attacks on its own in Arcadia but not here. The sword does have a +1 magic bonus and is effective against elementals. In a second ambush in [[Darvinblast]], opened with Sacred Flame at a Deep World priest (missed). Used Channel Divinity to heal [[Domyx]] after the heavy Blight damage. Practiced using his blade in combat — landing hits with Bless Strike but rolling low on damage. His summoned spirit (from Ceril's summoning) got additional attacks at end of turn.

In the triceratops encounter, cast Ice Ball (level 4) — decimated the remaining grunts who were already half-burnt from Red Caesar's Fireball. Cast Calm Emotions (level 2) on the wild triceratops — the beast failed its charisma save with a natural 0, immediately calming down. Used Nimbus Form to fly above the battlefield during the [[Fire Giant]] fight. Cast Toll the Dead on the Fire Giant from the air. Cast Healing Word on [[Domyx]] in the pit. Used Warding Flare to save Domyx from a lethal flame sword attack. Swooped down to rescue [[Zulu]] from the pit. Used Channel Divinity to heal Domyx (7 HP) during the short rest. Discovered he should have had three Channel Divinity uses since level 6.

During the church confrontation, attempted multiple charisma persuasion checks with the congregation, arguing for unity and freedom. Morel cast Flamestrike — Vokunar made his dex save for 25 fire damage. Morel cast Holy Word? — Vokunar used Guarded Mind (subclass feature) to resist, taking only 4 radiant damage instead of being stunned. Recognized that the defeated "Morel"'s face was different from the one seen through the illusion earlier. Cast Burning Hands (radiant) to finish off the weakened Morel. Consulted [[Aeris]] (Eris) via [[Ceril]]'s shield, asking five yes/no questions about the curse, the Penumbra, and the path forward. Eris confirmed destroying the spell was the right choice and that the Penumbra was nearby in the hills. Prepared Arcane Eye and Banishment after leveling.

**Level 8:** Took Heavy Armor Master feat (reduces slashing, bludgeoning, and piercing damage by 3). Rounding up strength score.
