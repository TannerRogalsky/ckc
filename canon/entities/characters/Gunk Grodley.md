A dapper goblin trader who captains the [[Goblin Traders]]' ship on the high seas. He wears a tall black top hat and a velvety outfit, and carries himself with merchantly confidence.

Gunk approached [[The Opal]] while the party was sailing from [[Brimbolyn]], offering to sell accessories, ammunition, and magical wares. He directed [[Kerben]] to his wife in the hold for poison crafting and sales. He also offered the rare scroll [[Subpoena Deuces Mercator]], which allows the buyer to summon the goblin traders anywhere at sea.
