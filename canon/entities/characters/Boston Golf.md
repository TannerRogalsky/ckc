A figure from the old acting troupe associated with [[The Carrot Cake]]. A broken fortune-teller machine in the [[Magic Hat]] contained a porcelain-skinned automaton labeled Boston Golf, inlaid with onyx, citrines, and rubies. [[Red Caesar]] noted the automaton's resemblance to [[Rochella Golf]], suggesting a family connection or shared lineage.
