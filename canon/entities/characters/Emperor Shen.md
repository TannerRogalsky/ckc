The emperor of [[Broy]], served by [[Vizier Jade]], who acts as his voice and agent.

In session 023, the party entered Jade's chamber and saw Emperor Shen slumped in a large glorious throne, his face obscured by metal and a headdress. He was old and barely moving. Jade revealed that Shen is [[Domyx]]'s grandfather — confirmed by the matching sky-colored palms shared between Domyx and the emperor, evidence of having touched the sky.

Shen spoke in Giant's Runic (understood by Domyx and Jade) and revealed that he was the first of his bloodline to touch the sky — "I was the first, my grandson, but you are the last." He planned to be the last sky-toucher, hence why he tried to destroy the sky using [[Starfall]]. He was not successful in destroying all of it. His goal, and the goal of the empire, is that "our rage will kill the gods." He believes the gods play with mortals like toys and that the suffering in the world is the gods' doing, not humanity's.

Shen's final words before falling unconscious were: "Stark will fall, but we will survive. The gods will die. We will live on." He pained himself to speak and collapsed from exhaustion.

In session 026, [[Vokunar]] scrys on Shen and witnesses him loading a large [[Penumbra]] chunk into [[Starfall]] and firing a beam of energy across the heavens. The beam strikes the moon, shattering it. Shen is now confirmed as the operator of Starfall who has attacked two of the three sister goddesses — first [[Aeris]] sixty years ago and now [[Crone]].
