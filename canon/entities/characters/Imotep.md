A yuan-ti associated with [[The League of New Stark]] and [[The White Drake]]. Serves as [[Lady Jacinthe]]'s second-in-command and head of transportation.

## Plot Events

In session 016, Imotep was summoned by Lady Jacinthe to help the party return from [[The White Drake]] to [[Lyngbakr Lagoon]]. He used the druid spell *Transport via Plants*, creating a temporary link between a plantain on the White Drake and its kind on the lagoon island, providing the party passage home.
