A gnomish armor forger in [[The Pit]] known for his grotesque appearance — he removed all his own skin and replaced it with metal. Last issued visas to the [[Gheister]]. [[Redrick Wenn]]'s dwarvish visa was stolen here.

In session 013, [[Beryzoz Helmscar]] sold [[Kerben]] the [[Vivarian Zodex]] (a +1 breastplate that grants +2 AC to nearby animal companions, with a rotating egg slot) and [[Red Caesar]] [[Beryzoz's Teeth]] — a ring made from his own teeth that grants necrotic resistance and once-per-day Speak with Dead. When the spell is cast, the teeth enter the corpse's mouth and speak through them.
