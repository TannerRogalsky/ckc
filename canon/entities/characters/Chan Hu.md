A merchant in the [[Broyish Capital]] market who claims to be the sole survivor of the pre-[[The Cataclysm]] world, with his soul carried forward across 17 successive forms. He operates a shop adorned with ancient-world artifacts and a large golden dragon statue. He is invisible to unworthy eyes and only appears to those he deems important.

Chan Hu previously knew [[Master Lee]] of [[Heaven's Bulb]] under the name "The Emerald Leaf." He recognized [[Red Caesar]] as a member of Heaven's Bulb and someone of importance. He trades in diamonds and legendary relics of the old world and dismissed the party's art pieces as insufficient for his wares.
