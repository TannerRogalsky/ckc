A human seneschal of the [[Broyish Empire]] who serves at the imperial palace. He intercepted the party at the palace entrance and verified their identities before granting them audience with [[Vizier Jade]] and [[Emperor Shen]]. He acts as Jade's entourage and gatekeeper to the imperial high court.

In session 024, [[Domyx]] encountered Faran near the Dawn Palace in the [[Broyish Capital]]. Faran explained that the Empire welcomes those who "fall through the cracks" in other societies — humans originally, then other races like titan folk (goliaths) who could not compete with elves and dwarves in their own lands. He noted that [[Clan Akathia]]'s nobility is well-known in the Empire, and that many titan folk have left their mountains and found opportunity in the capital. He told Domyx that "your blood, our blood, it's no different" and that Domyx is free to move around the city but must put in the work to achieve anything.
