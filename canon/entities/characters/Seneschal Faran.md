A masked human seneschal of the [[Broyish Empire]] who serves at the imperial palace. He intercepted the party at the palace entrance and verified their identities before granting them audience with [[Vizier Jade]] and [[Emperor Shen]]. He acts as Jade's entourage and gatekeeper to the imperial high court.
