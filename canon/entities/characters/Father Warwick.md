A high-ranking priest of [[Sigil]] who was petrified for approximately 70 years at the Temple of Sigil on Otyugh Isle.

Father Warwick survived [[The Cataclysm]] and was among a group of priests who had left [[Southport]] to establish a new temple in the new world. They made peace with local druids before being attacked by a cockatrice alongside invading Kuo-Toa, which petrified all 12 priests and 19 Kuo-Toa cultists present.

In session 025, [[Ceril]] identified him as the highest-ranking priest among the petrified statues and cast Greater Restoration to de-petrify him first. Warwick was an abjurer capable of removing curses and de-petrifying others himself, which allowed the party to efficiently restore several more priests. After being freed, Warwick planned to return to Southport to help combat the [[Mana Sickness]] plague and to attempt to convert the de-petrified Kuo-Toa to the faith of Sigil.
