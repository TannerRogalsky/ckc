Kerben's old boss from before [[The Cataclysm]]. Faraday is deceased; Kerben investigated his crypt and found tickets to [[The Carrot Cake]] inside, forming one of the breadcrumbs that eventually led the party to the amusement park.

Faraday was also [[Jack Harvey]]'s benefactor who wanted to make sure The Carrot Cake remained entertaining even after the apocalypse. He set up the wave-based combat system at [[King of the Hole]], where enemies are presented not by Jack Harvey but by Faraday's design. The system uses five beacons that must be activated in groups of three to trigger waves of enemies.

In session 030, a [[Magen]] clarified that Jack Harvey resigned from direct stewardship of [[The Carrot Cake]] and left the park in Faraday's care. Faraday was not a mortal being and did not age conventionally, making his long-term oversight of the park possible even after Jack's era ended.
