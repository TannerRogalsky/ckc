A figure at [[Heaven's Bulb]] who, alongside [[Keeper Rufus]], warned [[Red Caesar]] about the dangers of the world outside. Gave Red a set of two copper blades — [[Para and Bellum]]. Known personally by [[Vizier Jade]], who described him as "a good man." His current status is unknown.

In session 030, Master Lee appeared inside a magical version of one of [[Red Caesar]]'s recurring [[Heaven's Bulb]] memories. [[Vizier Jade]] and [[Emperor Shen]] intruded into that memory while searching Red Caesar's mind. Red Caesar moved to protect Master Lee and his younger self from Shen. After the intruders left, Master Lee asked Red Caesar to explain what had happened.
