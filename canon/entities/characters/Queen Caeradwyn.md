Former queen of the elves, killed roughly one century before the party's present. Her rule upheld a rigid aristocratic society in [[Brimbalin]] where most elves, and lower-status humans and dwarves, were enslaved. Her bloodline magic also cursed [[Castle Kaedon]] and drove the human king there insane; her death broke that curse and helped trigger revolutionary upheaval among the elves.

In session 015, [[Alamar]] revealed that the curse runs deeper than a simple bloodline enchantment. Queen Caeradwyn carried the blood of her people in its purest form and had an ambition that lasted centuries. After her death, female elves periodically rose up claiming to be her reincarnation, spreading a madness through [[Brimbalin]] and into other lands. This curse — physical, metaphysical, divine, and profane — has plagued Alamar his whole life and continues to manifest in the forces of hell and undead that now pervade [[Castle Kaedon]].
