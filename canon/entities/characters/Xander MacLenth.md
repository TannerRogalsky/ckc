A human pirate ghost found in the abandoned [[Far Helm Clan]] home. He and his pirate crew once tried to seize the coastal site from a dwarf family and were killed by the dwarves — likely including the ancestors of [[Gammix]] and [[Tammix]] whom the party befriended earlier. He lingered as a specter, waiting for the rising waters of [[The Cataclysm]] to sweep him away to the afterlife. When the waters rose too slowly, [[Vokunar]] used Create Water to raise the tide in the cave and release him. He was a "pirate through and through" who wanted to spend his wealth before his watery grave, unlike his legendary father [[Lenth the Rugged]] who wanted to leave a legacy. His family name is related to [[Pleasance MacLenth]].
