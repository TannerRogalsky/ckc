An elf mithril smith operating in the [[Elvish Marketplace]] of [[Brimbalin]], marked by two mithril statues at his shop entrance. He carries on the ancient Nokirna trade using a bluish-green flame to melt mithril quickly.

He crafted the [[Iklwa Isondo]] for [[Domyx]] — a +1 mithril trident with magnetic auto-return and pull effects linked to Domyx's lodestone greaves. The trade cost two uncommon items and a rare (gold-trimmed books and a chunk of pure gold) plus the mithril itself.
