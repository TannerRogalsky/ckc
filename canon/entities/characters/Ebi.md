A healer from [[Southport]] who joined [[The Opal]]'s crew alongside her husband [[Trent]].

Ebi is a skilled healer who cast Mass Cure Wounds upon meeting the crew. She offered to treat any injured or poisoned crew members and established herself in the ship's sickbay area. She proved particularly valuable to [[Domyx]]'s sister [[Lorelei]], who was pregnant and needed someone to monitor her health and discourage her from overexerting herself.

Ebi is likely a follower of [[Sigil]], given her healing focus. When the party returned near Southport, she disembarked with Trent to help cure the [[Mana Sickness]] plague and reunite with their child.
