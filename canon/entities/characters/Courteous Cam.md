A dwarf farmer and trader near [[The Pit]]. He traded dwarven seeds and a Call Lightning scroll to [[Ceril]] in exchange for a Plant Growth scroll and elvish darkwood bark. He is interested in elvish agriculture and the dwarven practice of redirecting lightning storms for power.

He also gave [[Ceril]] a map and the quest "Bounty in the Boneyard" — a lead to a [[Penumbra]] chunk in [[Gnoll]] territory to the north, guarded by gnolls who don't know its true value.
