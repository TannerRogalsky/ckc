An aasimar mentor to [[Vokenar]] in [[Arcadia]]. Suggested there are other paths than to become a Guardian and said "May Sigil's light guide you."

After the fall, she traveled to [[Brimbolyn]] to search for other Arcadian survivors. She now works in the [[Elvish Marketplace]], selling accessories and magical goods. She was one of [[Vokenar]]'s teachers at [[Academia Lux]] and recognized him immediately upon their reunion. She collects art from the old world and trades primarily for art pieces rather than gems.

She crafted a necklace for [[Ceril]] in exchange for a Mask of Ogun. The amulet, made of volcanic rock, allows Ceril to summon his familiar as a magma elemental — with claws, fire breath, and a death burst that explodes into a fireball if killed.
