A sailor from [[Southport]] who joined [[The Opal]]'s crew alongside his wife [[Ebi]].

Trent and Ebi fled Southport as the [[Mana Sickness]] plague spread through their city, threatening their children. They were recruited aboard [[The Opal]] by [[Kerben]] while serving as acting captain. Trent has experience sailing on multiple vessels, including briefly on a [[Broyish Empire]] ship. He was assigned as master gunner aboard The Opal and proved capable with the ship's cannons.

When the party returned near Southport after de-petrifying priests at the Temple of Sigil, Trent and Ebi disembarked to attempt to help cure the plague and reunite with their child. Trent had to transfer his master gunner duties to [[Ulrich Fjöler]] before departing.
