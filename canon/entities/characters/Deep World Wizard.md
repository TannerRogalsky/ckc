A mage encountered in [[Darvinblast]], allied with an [[Etten]] champion. Lives in a large house (described as a "duplex on the third floor") within the city. Commands the Etten (not via magical summoning but as a resident). Cast countespell (level 3) against [[Red Caesar]]'s magic missile, then cast Fireball (level 4) dealing 30 fire damage to Red and [[Ceril]]. Later attempted to grab [[Domyx]] with a force attack (24 force damage) and used Misty Step to retreat. Fought in melee with [[Kerben]] who dealt 33 damage, followed by [[Domyx]] finishing him. Cast Hellish Rebuke (level 2) as a dying attack, dealing 17 fire damage to Domyx (reduced to 8 via dex save). Described as skilled at magic and a tough combatant with many hit points.

A second Deep World Wizard was encountered in an ambush deeper in the city. This wizard cast Blight (level 4, 36 necrotic damage) on [[Domyx]], forcing a constitution save. Used Misty Step to reposition behind [[Vokenar]] to threaten [[Ceril]]. [[Red Caesar]] successfully Counterspelled his level 3 spell attempt. The wizard repeatedly failed to hit Red Caesar despite close proximity. Used Hellish Rebuke as a dying reaction when [[Domyx]] delivered the final blow. Like the first wizard, this one showed poor tactical learning — misty stepping directly beside [[Kerben]] proved fatal given Kerben's turn order. These wizards are part of a standardized training ("dwarven wizard schools") but show poor communication with each other in combat.
