Leader of the Duergar in [[The Deepworlders Delve]] at [[The Pit]]. The Duergar are obsessed with the [[Penumbra]] and are culty and insular. [[Figma Brickfinger's Union]] has an uneasy truce with them, though there was war in the past.

Also operates within the [[Darvinblast]] as a cult leader, exerting magical control over the dwarf population and brainwashing them into hostility toward the party. Broadcasts commands through the walls of the underground city via a booming voice system, rallying dwarf forces. His magical control was dispelled from the [[Steelfend Clan]] by [[Red Caesar]]'s level 4 Dispel Magic. The party was directed by divine consultation to find him before seeking the [[Penumbra]].
