A party member who favors kicking doors open and fighting with rage. Uses a halberd and has divine smite (Holy Light). Served as obstacle-course navigator in an earlier dungeon. Known for bold intimidation tactics, including grappling dwarf fighters and hurling them over garbage ravines. Has Cloud Jaunt (10-foot range), teleport javelin, magnetic boots, and Stoic Laurels. Travels aboard [[The Opal]]. Has a base movement speed of 67 feet, the highest on the team. Uses Recklessness and Divine Fury (choosing necrotic damage) with his halberd. Critically struck a [[Bat Rider]] with an uppercut move triggering Divine Fury. Uses his high mobility to position himself inside [[Ceril]]'s Moonbeam to hold enemies in place. Took heavy damage (17 points) from multiple grunts pouncing on him but survived through rage. Recruited [[Otto]] to join the crew alongside [[Vokunar]].
