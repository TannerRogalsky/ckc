A world-ending event that washed away much of the population and reset the world. Survivors like members of [[Heaven's Bulb]] emerged stronger and better. Occurred some time before the current events. [[Vokunar]] fell from the sky during or after this event. The cataclysm wiped clean many people who were "holding us back" according to [[Vizier Jade]]. A single person (the [[Crone]]) caused the cataclysm and achieved what they desired with it.
