A magical sash or championship belt awarded to [[Domyx]] after the party defeated every [[King of the Hole]] challenge, including the optional bonus wave.

The [[Magen]] announcer created the item by detaching one of its own rabbit tails, enchanting it, and presenting it to Domyx in deference. The item carries the arena's rabbit theme into a practical reward: it improves the wearer's leaping and can release a forceful shockwave when the wearer is knocked down or brought to the edge of defeat.

The sash does not require attunement.
