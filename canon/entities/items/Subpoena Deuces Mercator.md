A rare reusable spell scroll sold by [[Gunk Grodley]] of the [[Goblin Traders]]. When activated, it summons the goblin traders' ship to find the user anywhere at sea. The activation includes a sending-spell-style message, allowing the user to specify what items are needed.

[[The Opal]] acquired this item by trading one of their rare gems. The crew considers it an insurance policy and a potential travel-time shortcut — essentially a "goblin pager" for supply runs on the open ocean.
