A clear potion that lenses light differently when swirled, allowing the drinker to see further through the liquid. When consumed, it grants the effect of the clairvoyance spell for ten minutes without requiring concentration — the user can place a sensor within one mile to see or hear from that location. One of [[Lenth the Rugged]]'s innovations.
