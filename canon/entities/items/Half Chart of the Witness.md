One half of a map created by the construct [[Witness]] that charted the entirety of [[Stark]] before [[The Cataclysm]]. Found in the inner sanctum of the [[Southern Archipelago Castle]], revealed when [[Red Caesar]] cast Gust of Wind to clear black pollen from the walls. The map is incomplete — it contains only half the information spread across the full map, with some lines hidden or removed, making it both a map and a puzzle. It is unknown whether the other half exists or why Witness made two copies.
