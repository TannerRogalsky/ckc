A spell-storing shield crafted by [[Sigrid Forgewelt]] for [[Vokunar]], embedded with a ruby that glows to indicate stored magical power.

The shield starts with a random assortment of spells embedded within it. Vokunar can refresh stored spells during travel, making it especially useful for carrying situational magic.

Requires attunement.
