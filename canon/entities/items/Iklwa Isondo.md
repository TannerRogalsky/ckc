A +1 mithril trident crafted by [[Lysanderol Nokirna]] in the [[Elvish Marketplace]]. An iklwa is a thick African-style throwing/stabbing spear.

The weapon features a magnetic iron coil that links to [[Domyx]]'s lodestone greaves. When thrown, it automatically returns to Domyx's body. When pulled back, it can pull targets toward him as well. This replaces the topple effect on his previous trident. The mithril construction increases throw range from 20/60 to 40/100 feet. Domyx can also freely swap to and from the weapon.
