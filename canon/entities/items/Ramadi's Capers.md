A collectible comic series about the adventures of a dashing goblin rogue. Issue 4 was found unfurled on a bed in one of the [[Magic Hat]] hotel rooms. The comic has gold trim and is a periodical collectible. A collector exists who trades for the complete set, suggesting the full collection has greater value than individual issues.
