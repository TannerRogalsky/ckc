Magic cloaks that billow dramatically on command. They do not require attunement.

## Plot Events

In session 022, [[Red Caesar]] purchased Cloaks of Billowing for the entire party at the [[Elvish Marketplace]] in Brimbalin. The cost was two gold pieces for the whole set. Red described them as both an aesthetic and an "awesome" choice.
