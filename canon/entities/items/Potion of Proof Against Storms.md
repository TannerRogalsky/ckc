A potion tossed to [[Domyx]] by a mysterious dwarf from a window in [[Darvinblast]]. Grants resistance to both thunder and lightning damage for one hour after drinking. The dwarf who gave it spoke about courage, climbing, and questioning [[Morel Chainsunder]]'s plans — wondering if they were meant for all dwarves or only a privileged few.
