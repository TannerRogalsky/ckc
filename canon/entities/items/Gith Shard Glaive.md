A halberd crafted by [[Sigrid Forgewelt]] using a star gem from the [[Aeon]] encounter and a ruby. Named for the Githzerai origin of the star gem embedded in its structure.

The weapon excels at ranged cleave attacks: when Domyx uses its secondary cleave, the range extends to 30 feet instead of the normal 5 feet, allowing him to point the glaive and direct a bolt of psychic energy at distant targets. The cleave damage type changes to psychic when used at extended range.

Sigrid Forgewelt noted that crafting it required her understanding of strange magics and metals — the star gem's condensed space magic was integral to the weapon's design.
