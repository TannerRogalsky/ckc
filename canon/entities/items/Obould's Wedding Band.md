A wedding ring with an inscription, still in its original box lined with satin. It belonged to [[Obould]], who may have intended to give it to someone. It was among the few items Obould truly cared about when [[The Opal]]'s hold was broken into by the [[Xarag]]. The party recovered it from [[Xarag's Island]] in session 026 alongside the [[Orkland Pin of Courage]].
