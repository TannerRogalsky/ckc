A book kept in the possession of the [[Tome Keeper]] demon. Its full contents and powers are unknown, but the [[Rakshasa]] indicated that finding and killing the Tome Keeper — and presumably dealing with this tome — would remove one of the threats watching over him, allowing him to leave his hiding place at [[The Garden]].
