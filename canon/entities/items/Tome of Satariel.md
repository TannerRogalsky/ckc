A book kept in the possession of the [[Tome Keeper]] demon. Its full contents and powers are unknown, but the [[Rakshasa]] indicated that finding and killing the Tome Keeper — and presumably dealing with this tome — would remove one of the threats watching over him, allowing him to leave his hiding place at [[The Garden]].

## Session 023

[[Vokunar]] cast Legend Lore on the Tome of Satariel, revealing its nature: the Tome is made of 666 pages, each collected from a different wizard's soul — the [[Tome Keeper]] had to kill and extract the souls of 666 wizards to create it. The Tome is permanent and returns to hell if destroyed in the material realm. It captures souls of magical beings, and the Tome Keeper can only capture knowledge through death. The Tome resides in [[Tome Keeper's Pyramid]], a temple in the eastern waters whose interior mirrors its state in hell and contains traps and treacheries set by the Tome Keeper.
