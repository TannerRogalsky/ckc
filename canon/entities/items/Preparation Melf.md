A flask of sticky acid crafted by [[Kerben]] using his poisoner's kit and dragon scales harvested from the [[Xarag]] defeated at [[Castle Kaedon]]. The dragon's acid-resistant scales were processed into a throwable acid weapon.

When thrown at a target, the flask bursts into sticky, persistent acid that continues burning until the substance is removed.

Named "Preparation Melf" during the crafting process.
