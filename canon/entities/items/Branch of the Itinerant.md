A wand that serves as an empowered spellcasting focus. It requires attunement.

Additionally, when the wielder points the branch and draws a path, flowers bloom along that path.

## Plot Events

In session 022, [[Ceril]] acquired this wand at the [[Elvish Marketplace]] in Brimbolyn through [[Mudeep]]'s art-for-items trading system.
