A jet-black, oily poison sold by Gunk Grodley's wife aboard the [[Goblin Traders]]' ship. It weakens a poisoned target's ability to fight and can knock a vulnerable target unconscious.

[[Kerben]] traded his clutch of bullet eggs for this poison. The bullet eggs are a magical item that summons bullet creatures when activated — a defense mechanism the goblin trader's wife misunderstands, thinking they are cute pets rather than dangerous constructs.
