A rare framed landscape painting discovered by [[Domyx]] within [[Castle Kaedon]]. The artwork depicts what [[Brimbalin]] looked like before [[The Cataclysm]] — a lush, jungled continent with no visible water. The title "The Jewel of Alfheimer" is embossed on the frame.

The painting is autographed by its creator, [[Aramil Kiirnodel]], former leader of Brimbalin, whose signature appears at the bottom right. The artwork is considered a valuable rare item, likely of interest to the elves of Brimbalin who would want it returned.

The landscape matches areas the party later observed in Brimbalin — much of the city's terrain remained untouched and preserved as depicted in the painting.
