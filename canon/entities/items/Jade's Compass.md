A valuable seafarer's compass found by [[Kerben]] and [[Domyx]] inside [[Jack Harvey]]'s underwater vault in session 031. It is made with gold, jade inlays, rubies, electrum work, and a diamond reticle, with [[Vizier Jade]]'s name etched on the back.

The compass is water-damaged and not magical. Its presence inside the sealed vault suggests one of [[Vizier Jade]]'s agents, likely [[The Tyrant]], reached the vault years before the party and became trapped there.
