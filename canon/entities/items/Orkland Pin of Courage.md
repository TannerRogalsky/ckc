A pin of courage awarded to [[Obould]] for fighting in southern battles on the high seas. Obould earned it decades earlier — roughly 20 to 30 years ago — during the early skirmishes when he helped save orc tribes. It was among the items stolen from [[The Opal]]'s hold by the [[Xarag]] and recovered by the party from [[Xarag's Island]] in session 026.
