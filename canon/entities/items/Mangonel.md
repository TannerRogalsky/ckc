A mangonel (siege catapult) found at [[Castle Kaedon]]. It was originally a celebration device containing decorative fireworks from [[Broy]], with a weight-adjustment mechanism on the back that allows precise calibration of launch force. It was enchanted with a trap that triggered during the [[Xarag]] battle in session 018.

In session 019, [[Kerben]] used it to launch [[Domyx]] across an 80-foot gap to the southern island platform, adjusting the weight mechanism for his mass. The mangonel survived the stunt intact.
