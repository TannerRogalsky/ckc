A +2 falchion made from a megalodon tooth, given to [[Kerben]]. It grants +2 to attack and damage rolls. Its magical property allows the wielder to perceive the temperature of any visible water by sight alone — a trait inspired by shark behavior. The item is connected to [[Crone]]'s purview, given the link between moon, tides, and sharks.
