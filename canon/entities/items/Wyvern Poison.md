A potent injury-type poison crafted from wyvern glands, purified aboard the [[Goblin Traders]]' ship with the help of Gunk Grodley's wife.

[[Kerben]] traded an uncommon item and wyvern glands he had previously collected to create this poison. It can be applied as a bonus action before combat and persists on the weapon until its next use. Kerben also considered coating a bullet with it for delayed deployment.
