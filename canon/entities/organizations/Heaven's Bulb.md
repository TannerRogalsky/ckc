A seemingly unknown group to which [[Red Caesar]] belongs. Founded or led by [[Master Lee]] (also called Master Li). [[Keeper Rufus]] is another figure associated with it. Was active before and during [[The Cataclysm]]. Sought to make its members stronger and better — "capable of inheriting the new world." Its members who survived the cataclysm are notably stronger than ordinary humans. Described as having "cast out" its surviving members into the world. In a flashback, [[Master Lee]] and [[Keeper Rufus]] warned [[Red Caesar]] about the dangers of the world outside.
