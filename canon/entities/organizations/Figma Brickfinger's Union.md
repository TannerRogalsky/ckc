Also known as The Pitman's Union. A dwarven union operating in [[The Pit]]. Led by [[Figma Brickfinger]]. Runs [[The Palace of the Pit]]. Has an uneasy truce with the Duergar of [[The Deepworlders Delve]], though there was war in the past. The Duergar are obsessed with the [[Penumbra]] and are culty and insular. Collected a panicked dwarf who escaped the [[Darvinblast]] and surrendered himself to them.
