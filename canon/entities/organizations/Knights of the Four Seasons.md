The militia force that protects [[The Order of Seasons]] and its magi. Members wear cloaks of different colors but all bear the Order's symbol: a full moon at center with phases of the moon (gibbous, half, crescent) framing it like parentheses.

Stationed at the train junction leading into [[Brimbalin]], they inspect all arrivals, checking weapons and equipment. They cast Augury on travelers to determine safety and apply an augmented Sanctuary spell that prevents visitors from being attacked until they attack first. Led by [[Marshal Zem]]. On high alert due to [[Broyish Empire]] activity.
