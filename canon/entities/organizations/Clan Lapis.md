A titan folk clan known for mining and exploration. Unlike dwarven miners, the Lapis do not construct formal mines or tunnels — they enter hills and mountains to find precious gems without damaging the terrain, functioning more as explorers than industrial miners. The clan has a king.

[[Lorelei]], sister of [[Domyx]], married into this clan from [[Clan Akathia]].
