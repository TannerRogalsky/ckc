[[Domyx]]'s titan clan in the northeastern mountains. The clan believed [[Domyx]] was its first member to touch the sky, but [[Ulrich Fjöler]] discovered that another member of the bloodline had done so earlier and had been erased from the records.

In session 024, [[Domyx IV]] — Domyx's father and current clan ruler — enforced strict isolationist laws and had kept the truth about [[Domyx's Ancestor]] hidden from his children. When Domyx confronted him about the ancestor's crimes and refused to forget, Domyx IV disowned him, ending their relationship. [[Ulrich Fjöler]] was imprisoned and facing execution for discovering secrets about the clan's erased history. The clan's ruling seat is [[Akathia Manor]].
