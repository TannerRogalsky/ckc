A goblin crew that operates a trading ship with red sails and black markings. They sail the high seas of Thalasia, selling accessories, ammunition, potions, scrolls, and illegal goods — particularly poisons.

The crew is led by [[Gunk Grodley]], who handles general sales on deck. His wife works in the hold as a poison crafter and dealer. The crew also includes a goblin who manufactures bullets, arrows, and magical ammunition.

[[The Opal]] acquired the [[Subpoena Deuces Mercator]] scroll from them, allowing the party to summon the goblin traders to any point at sea within 2d4 days, along with a 15-word message.
