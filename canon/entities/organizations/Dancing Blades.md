A thieves guild that operated in old [[Kaedon]] before [[The Cataclysm]]. The guild had political representation through senators, and its members were powerful enough that senators would put their necks on the line to defend them.

The guild was significant enough that the [[Kaedon]] kingdom was attempting to root it out. [[Clockwork]] was a member who was imprisoned in [[Castle Kaedon]]'s dungeon at some point. A senator of the Dancing Blades was found among the dead prisoners in the castle cells, having been locked in during the rising waters of [[The Cataclysm]].
