A maritime organization led by [[Lady Jacinthe]]. Main ship is [[The White Drake]]. The party sought a Letter of Mark from the League to identify themselves as members in exceptional standing. The League flew a [[League Banner]] that can be divined by League members. Concerned about the [[Broyish Empire]].

In session 016, the League protected the party's hidden operations near [[Lyngbakr Lagoon]] by shooting down a [[Broyish Empire]] airship that had spotted the region. [[Damien Ouranous]] asked the party for information about the new island and [[Penumbra]] stones so the League could better defend them.

In session 031, [[Obould]] was rescued from [[Vizier Jade]] and [[Emperor Shen]], ending the hostage pressure the [[Broyish Empire]] had used to force [[Lady Jacinthe]] and the League into supplying or finding [[Penumbra]].
