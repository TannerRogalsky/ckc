A maritime organization led by [[Lady Jacinthe]]. Main ship is [[The White Drake]]. The party sought a Letter of Mark from the League to identify themselves as members in exceptional standing. The League flew a [[League Banner]] that can be divined by League members. Concerned about the [[Broyish Empire]].
