A dwarvish settlement in the northeastern dwarven continent. Seat of power for [[Figma Brickfinger's Union]]. The party was en route to the Pit when [[Vizier Jade]] visited [[Red Caesar]]. It represents civilization on this continent, as a panicked dwarf fled toward it after escaping the [[Darvinblast]].

### Notable Locations
- [[The Palace of the Pit]] — Seat of power, run by [[Figma Brickfinger's Union]]
- [[The Deepworlders Delve]] — Home of the Duergar, led by [[Morel Chainsunder]]
- [[The Funnel]] — Primary mining operation

### Notable People
- [[Figma Brickfinger]] — Leader of [[Figma Brickfinger's Union]] (The Pitman's Union)
- [[Morel Chainsunder]] — Leader of the Duergar in The Deepworlders Delve
- [[Sigrid Forgewelt]] — Dwarven weapon forger
- [[Beryzoz Helmscar]] — Gnomish armor forger; last issued visas to the [[Gheister]]

After [[Morel Chainsunder]]'s curse broke, [[The Pit]] faced unrest as deep worlders emerged from underground and other dwarves moved down into spaces that had been closed to them. [[Figma Brickfinger]] handled the political fallout while the party used the market, reinforced [[The Opal]], and departed.

## Session 022

[[The Opal]] returned to The Pit for ship upgrades. The city was under active industrial development: new wings were being added to [[The Palace of the Pit]], roads were being widened, and minecart tracks were being laid. Deep worlders were integrating into society with minor but manageable conflicts. Some deep worlders had set up shops in the market.

Scouts reported [[Broyish Empire]] airships in the region, though their presence near the Pit itself was unconfirmed. The party purchased a bulbous bow and hardened sails for [[The Opal]], and [[Domyx]] and [[Vokunar]] traded gems with [[Sigrid Forgewelt]] for new magical weapons.
