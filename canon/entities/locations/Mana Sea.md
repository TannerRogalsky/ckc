A treacherous body of water near [[Southport]]. The rising oceans transformed forests and land into swampy muck, creating conditions that harbor [[Mana Sickness]]. The sea carries not only beasts but also the plague that has spread to Southport's population. [[Deep Roses]] — the herbs needed to cure the sickness — grow exclusively underwater in the Mana Sea.
