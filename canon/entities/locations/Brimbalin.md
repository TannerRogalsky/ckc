An elfish continent with a magic city at its center. [[The Order of Seasons]] is believed to have traveled here after stealing a piece of [[Penumbra]]. The party plans to head here next to track them down.

In session 013, the party sailed along the coast and saw the light pollution of the inland city. The coastline is protected by cliffs and large-scale elven terrain-shaping. [[Sigil]] later indicated that [[The Order of Seasons]] had moved its [[Penumbra]] beyond the jungles to one of its most protected places.

Before [[Queen Caeradwyn]]'s fall, the surrounding elven lands were defined by aristocratic rule, magical terrain-shaping, and widespread slavery.
