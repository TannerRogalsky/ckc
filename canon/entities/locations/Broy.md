The eastern continent, home of the [[Broyish Empire]]. Ruled by [[Emperor Shen]]. Known for advanced technology including fast-moving flying machines (scouts capable of 40 mph). Uses white and gold as imperial colors. Also seeks [[Penumbra]] as a source of great energy and power.

The coastline features cliffs, mountainsides, waterfalls, and rushing rivers. The sky shows small holes from which waterfalls pour — remnants of the broken sky from [[The Cataclysm]].

The coastal approach to the [[Broyish Capital]] passes through an industrial steel town with whirring gears, copper electrical cables, and water-powered machinery. Winding stairs lead further up into the imperial capital. [[Oni]] sentries guard the approach at multiple positions, some invisible.

[[Kerben]] remains aboard [[The Opal]] guarding the ship while the rest of the party operates in the capital.
