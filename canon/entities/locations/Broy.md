The Empire in the eastern continent. Also known as the [[Broyish Empire]]. Ruled by [[Emperor Shen]]. Known for advanced technology including fast-moving flying machines (scouts capable of 40 mph). Uses white and gold as imperial colors. Also seeks [[Penumbra]] as a source of great energy and power. [[Vizier Jade]] promised the party safe passage when they eventually arrive. [[Lady Jacinthe]] of [[The League of New Stark]] is concerned about this empire hurting people under the League's protection. [[Boral Erendel]] hinted that "looking east" would reveal answers about imperial structure.
