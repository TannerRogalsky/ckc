An old-world human castle on a craggy island in the southern archipelago, still largely intact despite [[The Cataclysm]]. The castle was described to the party by members of [[The Garden]] as the former residence of [[Witness]], a construct who mapped all of [[Stark]]. After PAXO — a wicked new construct — appeared, the humans and Witness fled, and PAXO massacred the castle's inhabitants. The corridors are cramped and filled with a sooty black mist that harms intruders over time. The party cleared the castle using Gust of Wind, defeated PAXO, recovered the [[Half Chart of the Witness]], a [[Potion of Fluid Adamantite]], 50 pounds of mithril from the armory, and 100 pounds of [[Esperanto|Esperantan]] coins from a treasury hoard.
