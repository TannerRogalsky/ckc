The underground realm of the dwarves, also referred to as the deep world. It contains dozens of dwarf clans, including the [[Steelfend Clan]] and [[Far Helm Clan]]. Accessible through the [[Darvinblast]] and other entrances. The surface is concealed from above by large illusions (mirage arcana) covering the northern mountains. Dwarves here speak Northern. The realm features winding caves, magma veins, toxic smoke, and acrid fumes. Many clans remain hostile to outsiders.
