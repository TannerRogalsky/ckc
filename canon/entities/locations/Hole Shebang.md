An island of festivities, fun games, and entertainments within [[The Carrot Cake]], accessible from the midway through a teleport circle portal. Its portal is marked by a sign showing electricity arcing between two poles with a roller coaster in the background.

Inside, the portals take the form of barrels in a [[City Hole]] cavern. The area is described by a [[Magen]] as "a place filled with the technological marvels of the new world" that [[Jack Harvey]] was creating, with "lots of fun and games."

The island is subtropical with sandy beaches, a stage with automata puppets, and carnival games. Electrical arcs fire between poles across the island, powered by a central electrical hub with a large breaker switch. Flipping the switch deactivates the electrical traps and redirects power — activating one of the four lamps needed to open [[Jack Harvey]]'s inner sanctum. However, flipping the switch also spawns additional enemies.

The island features several carnival games including the "Sky High Striker" and "Al-Qadif's Tower of Treasures" on the outer island, and a "Smack a Bodak" whack-a-mole game and an eight-ball pool table in the interior area. Barrels throughout the area periodically spawn hostile enemies. A southern stage area has a backstage with curtains and boards that block line of sight.
