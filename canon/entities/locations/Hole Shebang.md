An island of festivities, fun games, and entertainments within [[The Carrot Cake]], accessible from the midway through a teleport circle portal. Its portal is marked by a sign showing electricity arcing between two poles with a roller coaster in the background.

Inside, the portals take the form of barrels in a small lantern-lit cavern. The area is described by a [[Magen]] as "a place filled with the technological marvels of the new world" that [[Jack Harvey]] was creating, with "lots of fun and games." It may now be infested with undead.
