The massive elfish continent and all that remains of the Old World. Heavily jungled, with a dense canopy and perpetual fog limiting visibility to 30-60 feet. The interior is protected by treacherous terrain, including sweltering jungle, bogs, and [[Akasha]] rainstorms that burn unprotected travelers.

The magic city of [[Brimbalin]] sits at the center of the continent. A system of overgrown train rails once connected disparate parts of the island, though much of the infrastructure is now buried and rusted. The rails were controversial even before [[The Cataclysm]], as their construction destroyed entire species and was seen as an affront to God and nature.
