One of four portal destinations in [[The Carrot Cake]], described by a [[Magen]] as a flying train that offers scenic overviews of [[Esperanto]] — the desert region [[Jack Harvey]] once called home. Its portal in the midway courtyard is marked by a sign showing a brightly colored wooden toy train on fake tracks. Its current state is unknown.
