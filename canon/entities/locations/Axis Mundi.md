A place beneath [[Stark]] at the center of the world. In session 031, [[The Tyrant]] revealed that [[Starfall]] is hidden there after [[Red Caesar]] charmed it inside [[Jack Harvey]]'s vault.

[[The Tyrant]] described Axis Mundi as the world's core: the place where [[Starfall]], formed from [[Stark]]'s four elements, is kept. The exact route there is unknown, but [[Red Caesar]] intends to seek help from [[Figma Brickfinger's Union]] to reach it from below.
