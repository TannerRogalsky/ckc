One of four portal destinations in [[The Carrot Cake]], described by a [[Magen]] as an island resort and coliseum area. Its portal in the midway courtyard is marked by a sign showing carrots flanking a hill. Its contents and current state are unknown — the magen warned the area may be infested with undead.
