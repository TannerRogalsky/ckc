A marketplace attached to the city of [[Brimbolyn]] where elves sell magical goods. [[Lysanderol Nokirna]] operates his mithril smithing shop here, marked by two mithril statues at the entrance. [[Gossa]] the aasimar sells accessories and magical goods in the market.
