An island on the party's route through the southern waters. [[The Opal]] was once attacked near Zerag's Island by the larger [[Black Dragon]] from the prologue, and treasure from The Opal remains sunken in the dragon's hoard there. After leaving the [[Southern Archipelago Castle]] in session 025, the party continued toward Zerag's Island to confront the dragon and recover the ship's lost treasure.
