A lagoon on [[Otyugh Isle]] near the approaches to [[Castle Kaedon]] and [[Lyngbakr]]. After receiving [[Penumbra]], [[Alamar]] developed it into a small base with shelters, water storage, torches, and an irrigation chute for feeding stones to [[Lyngbakr]]. The party left [[Kilosaurus]], [[Relic Elnavi]], and three deckhands here to help defend and develop the settlement.
