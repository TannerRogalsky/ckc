A realm above Stark, possibly a plane of existence. [[Vokenar]] originated from a citadel here 60 years ago before falling through the sky toward Stark. [[Solar Flare]] and [[Gossa]] were encountered here. [[Lyngbakr]] fell from Arcadia through the [[Hole in the Sky]].

[[Emperor Shen]] may have attempted to reach Arcadia by [[Touching the Sky]], going beyond the rite's usual purpose of simply touching the sky.

In session 031, [[Lyngbakr]] repaired the [[Hole in the Sky]] and passed into the heavens with [[Ceril]] and [[Alamar]] as the sky sealed beneath them. [[Vokenar]] also resisted [[Vizier Jade]]'s attempt to banish him back to Arcadia, choosing to remain on [[Stark]].
