The human city that surrounded and led up to [[Castle Kaedon]] in the age before [[The Cataclysm]]. Associated with the "evil empire in the west" that the humans built.

After [[Lyngbakr]] rose carrying the castle, the sunken remains of Westerness became visible beneath the waters surrounding the island. Streets, buildings, and ancient woodland formations can be seen through the water — nature has reclaimed the city, with limed-over trees and branches of an ancient woodland. For [[Red Caesar]], seeing it is like looking into history books. [[Kerben]] had visited Westerness in its time, while [[Ceril]] knew of the city but had not been there.
