The imperial capital of the [[Broyish Empire]], located in the hills above the industrial steel town of [[Broy]]. The city is a highly industrialized metropolis with automated wheeled devices powered by magic and electricity, copper cables carrying electrical arcs, whirring gears, and rushing water powering machinery. Airships are common overhead. The city features a large market with diverse non-human residents despite the empire's xenophobic reputation, and a palace where [[Vizier Jade]] holds [[Obould]] prisoner.

The city is guarded by [[Oni]] sentries positioned on pillars and rooftops, some of which are invisible and rotate positions at set intervals. Magic is restricted in certain zones — permanent dead magic areas in hotels and Globe of Invulnerability-like fields that suppress spells below 7th level. Spell scrolls are contraband. Citizens can be scryed upon more easily than visitors, and citizenship papers can be fabricated by underground fixers.
