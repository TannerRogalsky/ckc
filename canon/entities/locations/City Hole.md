The central hub area of [[The Carrot Cake]]'s inner zones, accessed from the midway courtyard through a portal. It is a small interior cavern lit by electrically powered copper lanterns — technological rather than magical. Portals here take the form of barrels, each labeled with the name of a destination: the [[Hole Shebang]], the [[Magic Hat]], [[King of the Hole]], and [[Hole on Wheels]]. The barrel the party arrived from is labeled "City Hole" and returns to the midway.

A wireframe gate at the far end of the cavern leads deeper into the zone. [[Red Caesar]]'s investigation revealed that the portals function as teleport circles, and that "City Hole" is a pun on "City Hall."
