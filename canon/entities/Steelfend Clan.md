A dwarf clan operating in the [[Darvinblast]]. The clan includes [[Gammix]], [[Tammix]], and their family (women, children, and other brothers). Their clan house occupies an entire wing of the deep world complex. The matron of the family speaks Western and was grateful to the party, particularly [[Red Caesar]], for dispelling [[Morel Chainsunder]]'s magical control over them. They gifted the party with deep world coinage after being freed.
