## Chunks

### Chunk 0000

The party splits into two groups to pursue parallel objectives. [[Kerben]], [[Domyx]], and [[Vokunar]] sail [[The Opal]] to [[The Pit]] for ship upgrades. [[Red Caesar]] and [[Ceril]] use Teleportation Circle to travel to [[The White Drake]] to seek information about [[Obould]]'s fate.

At [[The Pit]], [[Brim the Bullywog]] hops off the boat to explore Dwarfland independently, never having been there with so much room to move. The party visits the marketplace where [[Domyx]] trades a Gith Star Gem, a ruby, and gold to [[Sigrid Forgewelt]] for the [[Gith Shard Glaive]], a halberd with extended-range psychic cleave attacks. Domyx also trades equestrian manuals for enchanted javelins. [[Vokunar]] trades a rare gem for [[Sunset's Solace]], a spell-storing shield. The party purchases ship upgrades including a bulbous bow and hardened sails.

[[Vokunar]] visits [[Figma Brickfinger]] at [[The Palace of the Pit]]. She reports that deep worlder integration is progressing with minor conflicts, industrial expansion is underway (new palace wings, widened roads, minecart tracks), and aerial scouts have sighted [[Broyish Empire]] airships in the region.

At [[The White Drake]], [[Damien Ouranous]] greets [[Red Caesar]] and [[Ceril]]. He reports that [[Lady Jacinthe]] is inconsolable and will not receive visitors. Through Damien, she delivers a devastating message: "For all intents and purposes, you should treat your captain as deceased. There is no more Captain Obould. He is dead and gone." Invoking his name caused Jacinthe to weep extensively. The party is given lodging in room 17.

[[Ceril]] infiltrates the ship's interior, blending in with refugees and staff. He notes heavy security around Jacinthe's quarters and instead tracks down the laundry service. He steals clothes, disguises himself as a crew member named "Baragas," and tricks the frazzled half-orc sommelier into handing over a prized bottle of Talyn brandy that Jacinthe had been drinking from.

Back in their room, [[Ceril]] uses the brandy bottle as a focus to attempt his first scrying on Jacinthe, but Jacinthe resists. [[Red Caesar]] suggests finding a portrait of Jacinthe for a second attempt with better odds.

### Chunk 0001

[[Red Caesar]] creates a loud distraction in the lounge while [[Ceril]] steals a portrait of [[Lady Jacinthe]] christening [[The White Drake]]. [[Damien Ouranous]] helps Ceril conceal the painting with his cape.

[[Ceril]] successfully scried Jacinthe through the portrait, revealing her grief was a ruse. [[Vizier Jade]] appeared in Jacinthe's room and delivered a one-month ultimatum: help the [[Broyish Empire]] find [[Penumbra]] or [[Obould]]'s safety could not be guaranteed. Jacinthe's whispered reaction — "One month. Can we even get there in one month?" — confirmed she wants Obould to survive.

[[Red Caesar]] and [[Ceril]] left a message for Damien hidden in the painting, then used a teleport circle to travel to [[Brimbalin]]. They arrived at [[House Kiernodel]]'s courtyard, where [[Risolvir Kirnodell]] and [[Veronia Karadel]] recognized them. Red reported that [[Boral Erendel]] had gone off the rails at [[The Garden]].

At the [[Elvish Marketplace]], Red trades [[The Jewel of Alfheimer]] to [[Mudeep]] for the [[Boots of the Alvigard]]. Ceril acquires the [[Branch of the Itinerant]], a flowering spellcasting wand. Red purchases [[Cloaks of Billowing]] for the entire party.

The party reunites near [[The White Drake]] and debriefs [[Domyx]], [[Kerben]], and [[Vokunar]] about the Jade ultimatum. [[Vokunar]] performs two lore divinations: one on [[Genesis Mundi]], confirming it is a natural world-reset event that the [[Demi-Spell]] merely expedites and modifies; and one on [[Starfall]], confirming it was created in hell, brought to [[Broy]], and uses [[Penumbra]] as fuel. The party now has approximately 21 days remaining before Jade's deadline expires.

[[David Harvey]], a [[Harengon]], approached [[Domyx]] with a quest: retrieve the traitor [[Theo Harvey]] from imperial-occupied territory. Theo betrayed the Harengon by revealing their underground warrens to the [[Broyish Empire]]. The League has assembled a large gold bounty for his safe return. David's great uncle was [[Jack Harvey]], creator of [[The Carrot Cake]].

The party discussed their options: the harengon quest, heading east toward the [[Broyish Empire]] to rescue Obould, or other side quests. They leaned toward taking the harengon quest as it could introduce underground shortcuts.

### Chunk 0002

The party sails [[The Opal]] to the [[Harengon Warrens]] island to capture [[Theo Harvey]]. The island is dotted with thick baobab-like trees and feels oddly empty. [[Red Caesar]] detects a Harengon nearby.

The party is ambushed by invisible imperial scouts and marshals working alongside Theo. After a battle, Red traps Theo alive. The remaining imperial forces attempt to flee by longboat, but [[Ceril]] and [[Vokunar]] stop them, killing most of the remaining soldiers.

[[Vokunar]] locates the entrance to the Harengon warrens — a hole beneath a baobab tree concealed by an illusion panel of wood. The dirt that had been moved to create the entrance had also been made invisible.

While captive, Theo revealed that [[David Harvey]]'s plan to move the Harengon into the underground warrens was doomed since the waters keep rising. He also revealed that [[Jack Harvey]]'s final project — [[The Carrot Cake]] — is "alive and well," implying the amusement park or its magical legacy still exists somewhere. Theo tried to persuade the party to take him to the [[Broyish Empire]] first rather than turning him over to the League, arguing he could serve as an inside contact.

The party decides to skip [[Cutlass Cray]] and continue east toward the [[Broyish Empire]] with Theo as prisoner, weighing his value as a bargaining chip against the risk of him escaping or manipulating the crew.

### Summary

The party split into two groups, pursuing separate objectives at [[The Pit]] and [[The White Drake]]. At The Pit, [[Domyx]] and [[Vokunar]] acquired powerful new equipment from [[Sigrid Forgewelt]], and [[Vokunar]] learned from [[Figma Brickfinger]] that deep worlder integration was progressing and [[Broyish Empire]] airships were being sighted in the region. At The White Drake, [[Lady Jacinthe]] declared [[Obould]] deceased. [[Ceril]] infiltrated the ship and, after an initial failed scrying through Jacinthe's brandy bottle, succeeded in a second attempt using a stolen portrait. The scrying revealed Jacinthe's grief was a ruse and that [[Vizier Jade]] was coercing her with a one-month ultimatum tied to Obould's survival. The party then traveled to [[Brimbalin]] via teleport circle, acquired new equipment from the [[Elvish Marketplace]], and received a quest from [[David Harvey]] to retrieve the traitor [[Theo Harvey]]. [[Vokunar]]'s lore divinations confirmed key details about [[Genesis Mundi]] and [[Starfall]]. The party now has approximately 21 days to act before Jade's deadline expires.

The party sailed to the [[Harengon Warrens]] island and was ambushed by [[Theo Harvey]] alongside imperial scouts and marshals. [[Red Caesar]] trapped Theo alive. [[Ceril]] and [[Vokunar]] eliminated most of the fleeing imperial forces. The party discovered the hidden entrance to the Harengon warrens beneath a baobab tree. Theo revealed that [[The Carrot Cake]] is "alive and well," while Red identified distinctive imperial markings on the soldiers. The party skipped [[Cutlass Cray]] and continued east toward the [[Broyish Empire]] with Theo as prisoner.
