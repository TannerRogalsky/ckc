# Session 011

## Events

- **Flashback — Vizier Jade's visit:** About a week earlier, [[Vizier Jade]] appeared on the deck of [[The Opal]] at night and spoke with [[Red Caesar]]. She revealed she serves [[Emperor Shen]] of [[Broy]] and knows of [[Heaven's Bulb]] and the [[Penumbra]]. She urged Red to travel east to the Empire, promised him safe passage, and warned that his crewmates are liabilities. She vanished via Teleport. Red kept this secret from the party.
- **Flashback — Vokunar's training:** Forty years ago, a young [[Vokunar]] was trapped in stone in a place outside of time. [[Crone]] and [[Sigil]] trained him to be a weapon for an inevitable war, having him fight elemental constructs. Vokunar has no memory between training sessions. The goddesses estimate he has a few more decades, possibly years, before his fall ends.
- **Present — Imperial scout:** While the party entered the [[Darvinblast]], [[Ceril]] kept watch outside and spotted an imperial flying machine scouting the area. He sent [[Nyquil]] to tail it; the owl followed it 25+ miles before it flew out over the sea. Ceril also discovered a large *Mirage Arcana* illusion concealing something in the northern mountains.
- **Present — Fleeing dwarf:** A panicked, injured dwarf burst from the Darvinblast entrance and fled toward the Pit. Nyquil followed him and reported that he surrendered himself to the [[Figma Brickfinger's Union]] in town.
- **Entry into Darvinblast:** The party descended through collapsing back doors and toxic fumes into the underground dwarven city. [[Vokunar]] cast Create Water and [[Red Caesar]] used Shape Water to protect against magma splashes. Kerben drew on his childhood poison resistance to the deep world fumes.
- **First encounter in the atrium:** The party entered a large public square where the dwarf population was sealed into wall residences. [[Morel Chainsunder]]'s voice boomed through the walls, rallying dwarf fighters. The party faced grunts, spies, priests, and [[Bat Rider]]s. [[Vokunar]] cast Calm Emotions on the party to counter the fear/anger of being surrounded. Some dwarves recognized Red from his earlier dispel magic.
- **Diplomatic overtures:** [[Domyx]] attempted intimidation by grappling a dwarf and hurling him back into the walls rather than killing him. [[Kerben]] shouted in Northern demanding the party be taken to Morel Chainsunder. The dwarves were confused but remained hostile.
- **Combat — First wave:** The party fought through multiple dwarf combatants. Red cast a level 5 Lightning Bolt through a line of enemies, killing a priest. [[Vokunar]] healed the party with Aura of Vitality. [[Kerben]] was backstabbed by a spy for significant poison damage but survived. [[Ceril]] used Woe extensively against a powerful priest's radiant attacks.
- **Combat — Second wave (atrium):** A new wave of enemies emerged including more [[Bat Rider]]s, grunts, spies, and a priest. [[Gammix]] and [[Tammix]] (previously freed dwarves) joined the party's side, fighting with heavy crossbows. [[Ceril]] cast Moonbeam (level 2), creating a crescent moon image of Crone on the ceiling. [[Domyx]] used his high mobility to position inside the Moonbeam and hold enemies in place. [[Red Caesar]] gained and used *Resilient Sphere* to create an impenetrable warding bubble. [[Vokunar]] healed the party extensively over multiple rounds.
- **Battle conclusion:** The party defeated all enemies in approximately six rounds. [[Gammix]] and [[Tammix]] chose to leave the party and stay with their clan.
- **Reunion with Steelfend Clan:** The freed dwarves led the party to the [[Steelfend Clan]]'s hidden house. The clan matron spoke Western, thanked the party (especially Red for dispelling [[Morel Chainsunder]]'s control), and gave them deep world coinage.
- **Map discovery:** [[Ceril]] found a map to the [[Far Helm Clan]] keep among the effects of a defeated enemy — a cavern where the clan hid treasure as insurance.
- **Short rest:** The party rested in the clan's wing of the complex while other clans remained hostile outside.

## Items Acquired

- Deep world coinage (from the Steelfend Clan matron)
- Map to the [[Far Helm Clan]] keep (found by Ceril among enemy effects)

## Characters & Plot Summary

The session opened with two flashbacks establishing major plot threads. [[Vizier Jade]]'s visit to [[Red Caesar]] introduces the Empire of [[Broy]] as a faction interested in the party's quest for [[Penumbra]], and sets up a potential journey east after the dwarven arc concludes. She specifically warned Red that his crewmates are liabilities — a tension that hasn't yet manifested.

[[Vokunar]]'s training flashback reveals he is being prepared by [[Crone]] and [[Sigil]] as a weapon for an unknown but inevitable war. He has no memory between sessions and doesn't know what "wrong" he's meant to right. The third goddess [[Aeris]] was also heard speaking, though her role remains unclear.

In the present, the party entered the [[Darvinblast]], an underground dwarven city hidden beneath a massive illusion in the northern mountains. The city is under the magical control of [[Morel Chainsunder]], a cult leader who brainwashed the dwarf population into hostility. The party's strategy was to minimize casualties — [[Vokunar]] repeatedly healed defeated dwarves to demonstrate goodwill, [[Domyx]] threw enemies back into their homes rather than killing them, and [[Kerben]] shouted demands in Northern.

The combat in the Darvinblast was the longest of the session, spanning two major encounters in the atrium. The party recruited two dwarves ([[Gammix]] and [[Tammix]]) who had been previously freed by Red's dispel magic. After the battles, the [[Steelfend Clan]] was fully liberated and chose to remain in the deep world.

The discovery of the Far Helm Clan treasure map and the imperial scout's interest in the region set up hooks for future sessions. The party is now resting within the Darvinblast, with Morel Chainsunder still at large and the [[Penumbra]] still to find.
