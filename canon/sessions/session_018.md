## Session 018

### Chunk 0000

- The party scouts the southern perimeter of the Castle Kaedon island after a short rest near the granary.
- [[Ceril]] spots specters rising from a graveyard field hidden in fog, alerting the party before the ambush can spring.
- Behind the party, water seeping from old cisterns collects into a vortex that forms a water elemental, trapping the party between two threats.
- [[Vokenar]] uses Turn Undead to devastate the specter pack, leaving them frightened and incapacitated. Most flee back into their graves.
- [[Domyx]] engages the water elemental, hampering its movement and holding it away from the rest of the party.
- [[Red Caesar]] uses Gust of Wind to push specters toward the cliff edge. [[Kerben]] shoots at the elemental.
- [[Domyx]] is grappled and pulled inside the water elemental's vortex form, where he continues attacking from within despite crushing pressure.
- [[Vokenar]] disintegrates the water elemental, reducing it to silvery gray nothing that fades from the plane and freeing Domyx.
- [[Ceril]] finishes the last specter. The party takes a short rest while [[Kerben]] resummons a fresh [[Tango]] and [[Domyx]] recovers from the specters' life drain.
- The party explores a building behind the granary, finding horse racks, barrels, and old leather. The building has four entrances.
- [[Red Caesar]] enters through the back door and triggers a brown mold infestation that spreads frostbite. [[Ceril]] uses Shape Water combined with create water to freeze and destroy the mold entirely.
- [[Red Caesar]] finds an old [[Kaedon]] equestrian manual — a collector's item in a world where few people know what horses look like.
- [[Ceril]] wild shapes into a scorpion and squeezes under the granary door to scout the interior, discovering [[Sturges]] hanging from the ceiling.
- The party devises a "hamster ball" strategy: [[Red Caesar]] encases himself in Otiluke's Resilient Sphere and enters the granary to attract sturges while [[Kerben]] creates blood scent from another entrance.
- Ochre jellies emerge from grain silos inside the granary. [[Vokenar]] uses an icy Flame Strike from outside, catching both sturges and jellies.
- The party systematically defeats the sturges and ochre jellies. [[Ceril]] uses Moonbeam and Wild Resurgence. [[Domyx]] brute-forces through the door to engage directly.

### Chunk 0001

- The party finishes clearing the sturges in the granary and searches the surrounding area.
- Under dead mold in a corner, a corpse of a human female noble from old [[Kaedon]] is found. She had been wearing noble gowns and was frostbitten to death.
- [[Red Caesar]] uses [[Beryzoz's Teeth]] (Speak with Dead) to question the corpse, learning several key facts:
  - She died when something fell on her during the castle's collapse, preceded by streaks of light from the east.
  - **[[Alamar]]** revolutionized western Stark, abdicated the old crown, and led to the creation of the senate — transforming Kaedon into a republic of syndicates.
  - She had tickets for **[[The Carrot Cake]]**, a massive amusement park spanning multiple mountaintops using great magic, set up by Jack Harvey.
  - She lived in **[[Taylin]]**, a settlement not far from the castle, and was visiting as a final trip before relocating to [[Esperanto]].
  - [[Esperanto]] was the lowest point on the land and was likely flooded first during [[The Cataclysm]].
- The streaks of light from the east match what [[Ceril]] saw in his pre-Cataclysm flashback and what [[Vokenar]] witnessed — they may be the direct cause of [[The Cataclysm]].
- The party explores the castle's cliff edges: smooth white cliffs descending 35 to 50 feet to the ocean, with broken landmasses on barnacle-encrusted pillars.
- [[Kerben]] uses Stone Sense to find climbing paths and discovers hollow underground spaces — much of the castle is buried beneath the surface.
- The party is ambushed along the cliffs by two water elementals rising from the ocean and [[Animated Armor]] constructs disguised as statues.
- [[Red Caesar]] destroys an animated armor with Dispel Magic, confirming they are sustained by active spellwork. [[Ceril]] does the same to a second.
- [[Vokenar]] uses Wall of Ice to slow the water elementals. [[Kerben]] snipes elementals from range, shattering one partially frozen target.
- [[Domyx]] uses brutal strikes and the [[Iklwa Isondo]] to help finish the remaining enemies, including psychic goops.
- After a short rest, the party scouts forward along the cliff edges toward more broken landmasses.
- A [[Xarag]] and four black dragon wyrmlings burst from the ocean waters and attack. [[Ceril]] recognizes them as offspring of the dragon from the prologue.
- [[Vokenar]] opens with Sacred Spear, striking all five dragons. [[Domyx]] uses the [[Iklwa Isondo]] to kill wyrmlings.
- [[Red Caesar]] attempts Charm Monster on the adult black dragon but fails. The dragon uses acid breath, damaging [[Kerben]] and melting his cover.
- [[Ceril]] creates a Wall of Fire, trapping the adult dragon in a corridor. The dragon declares "father will be pleased" when it finds the castle.
- The battle continues with the dragon weakened but still active inside the fire cage.

### Chunk 0002

- The [[Xarag]] battle concludes. [[Kerben]] triggers a hidden mangonel trap during the fight before its enchantment fades.
- The adult dragon's acid breath overwhelms [[Ceril]]'s starry shield and knocks Ceril unconscious.
- [[Vokenar]] heals Ceril with Aura of Vitality, restoring him as [[Domyx]] delivers the final blow to the adult black dragon. As the dragon falls, Vokenar loses his wings and Domyx shrinks back down.
- After the battle, [[Kerben]] harvests the dragon's acid-resistant scales and crafts [[Preparation Melf]] — a throwable sticky acid flask.
- The party decides not to rest and continues investigating the area.
- Investigation reveals decorative fireworks from [[Broy]] stored in the mangonel — it was originally a celebration device, not a weapon.
- [[Domyx]], while recovering and wiping dust off a wall, discovers [[The Jewel of Alfheimer]] — a rare framed landscape painting by [[Aramil Kiirnodel]] depicting what [[Brimbolyn]] looked like before [[The Cataclysm]] as a lush jungled continent with no visible water.
- The north door of the room is arcane-locked, but [[Kerben]] has magical keys that can open it.

### Summary

The party continues exploring the risen [[Castle Kaedon]], moving to the southern perimeter of the island near the granary area. The exploration is immediately met with danger — specters rise from a hidden graveyard field, and a water elemental forms from seeping cistern water behind them. [[Ceril]]'s keen perception saves the party from a full ambush.

[[Vokenar]]'s Turn Undead proves devastating against the specters, clearing most of them in a single use. The water elemental becomes the primary threat, engaging [[Domyx]] and eventually pulling him inside its vortex form. Despite the pressure and grapple, Domyx continues attacking from within until Vokenar disintegrates the creature entirely.

After a short rest, the party investigates a building behind the granary. [[Red Caesar]] triggers a brown mold hazard that spreads frostbite, and [[Ceril]] uses Shape Water and create water to freeze the mold. Red finds an old [[Kaedon]] equestrian manual, a rare artifact in a world where horses are largely unknown.

The party then scouts the granary interior. [[Ceril]] wild shapes into a scorpion to peek under the door, discovering [[Sturges]] hanging from the ceiling. The party executes a creative "hamster ball" strategy: [[Red Caesar]] encases himself in Otiluke's Resilient Sphere and enters the room to bait the sturges, while [[Kerben]] creates blood scent to draw them toward the sphere. Ochre jellies emerge from grain silos, but [[Vokenar]]'s icy Flame Strike and the party's coordinated attacks clear the room.

After clearing the sturges, the party discovers a preserved corpse of a [[Kaedon]]-era noble woman under dead mold. [[Red Caesar]]'s Speak with Dead reveals crucial lore: [[Alamar]] transformed Kaedon from monarchy to republic; [[The Carrot Cake]] was a massive magical amusement park spanning mountaintops; the settlement of [[Taylin]] existed nearby; and streaks of light from the east preceded the castle's collapse. These streaks match what [[Ceril]] and [[Vokenar]] witnessed, suggesting they may be the direct cause of [[The Cataclysm]].

The party explores the castle's cliff edges, where [[Kerben]]'s Stone Sense reveals hollow underground spaces. They are ambushed by water elementals rising from the sea and [[Animated Armor]] constructs. After clearing these threats and resting, the party is attacked by a [[Xarag]] and four wyrmlings that burst from the ocean. The adult dragon declares "father will be pleased" when it finds the castle, hinting at a larger draconic threat.

The dragon battle concludes with [[Domyx]] delivering the killing blow after [[Vokenar]] restores [[Ceril]] to consciousness. [[Kerben]] triggers a hidden mangonel trap during the fight but survives. Afterward, Kerben harvests the dragon's scales to craft [[Preparation Melf]], a throwable acid flask. The party discovers decorative fireworks from [[Broy]] in the mangonel — evidence of the castle's peaceful past. [[Domyx]] finds [[The Jewel of Alfheimer]], a rare painting by [[Aramil Kiirnodel]] showing Brimbolyn before the Cataclysm.
