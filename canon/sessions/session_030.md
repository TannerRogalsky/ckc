## Session 030

### Chunk 0000

The party continued the [[King of the Hole]] arena from the previous session, pressing the fourth beacon combination. The wave split the battlefield again: [[Vokenar]] and [[Red Caesar]] fought a [[Gorgon Head]] and [[Phase Cat]]s on one side of the island, while [[Ceril]] and [[Domyx]] faced [[Hooksy the Clown Automaton]] variants and another gorgon head near the lighthouse.

The gorgon heads repeatedly threatened petrification. Ceril dispelled the stone creeping over his body, then dove from the lighthouse using a feather fall potion so he could descend quickly without being removed from the fight. Vokenar had to spend divine magic reversing petrification on himself while keeping Red Caesar protected. Domyx was nearly overwhelmed by Hooksy variants but kept fighting long enough for Ceril's healing to stabilize the southern side.

The party defeated the fourth required wave. After regrouping on the hilltop, the [[Magen]] announcer declared that the final bonus challenge would be a king-of-the-hill defense on the pinnacle: the party had to remain on the top of the hill while the arena illusion made the surrounding ocean seem to rise around them.

### Chunk 0001

The party accepted the optional bonus challenge. The final wave brought [[Smoke Elemental]]s, [[Shambling Mound]]s, and [[Death Spark]]s against the hilltop. [[Red Caesar]] used Bigby's Hand and broad offensive magic, [[Vokenar]] burned light outward from inside a shambling mound, [[Ceril]] summoned another bestial spirit, and [[Domyx]] used cloud jaunt to swap places with Ceril and pull him free from engulfing vines. The party defeated every challenge in King of the Hole.

For the bonus victory, the magen announcer granted [[Domyx]] the [[Hopping Mad Sash]], a magical belt or sash made from the rabbit-magen's own detached and enchanted tail. After receiving the prize, the party returned to the midway and found all but one lamp lit.

[[Tango]] arrived with a hastily written message from [[Kerben]]: speak with Tango. Ceril cast Speak with Animals and learned that [[The Opal]] had been attacked by multiple boats. Kerben arrived in time to help fight off the first wave, but a larger [[Broyish Empire]] force was approaching. Tango warned that the shattered moon leaves every night dark, making it easier for the Empire to sneak around the elves and [[The Order of Seasons]]. Kerben remained aboard The Opal to hold the attackers back while the party continued toward the last lamp.

The party asked the magen about [[Hole on Wheels]] and [[Jack Harvey]]. The magen described Hole on Wheels as a scenic train once overlooking [[Esperanto]]. It also explained that Jack Harvey eventually resigned and left the park in [[Faraday]]'s care. Faraday was not mortal and did not age conventionally.

Pressed for time, the party used healing magic and entered Hole on Wheels. The portal emerged inside a moving train whose paintings served as teleport circles back to the other park areas. Ceril's owl familiar scouted the train exterior and reported seven cars moving in a broad loop over the ocean, with vampire-controlled enemies aboard and the final lamp switch near the front.

### Chunk 0002

The party advanced through Hole on Wheels as a group. They found two tins of preserved carrot tea and a scroll with two Mass Healing Word inscriptions behind the first bar. A fight erupted with cambions, revenants, vampire familiars, and vampiric spawn. Vokenar cast a blade barrier down the length of the train, tearing holes through the ceiling and forcing the enemies to scatter. Ceril's summoned crab helped clear enemies in the cramped cars while Domyx held the front and Red Caesar protected him from domination.

While moving forward, Red Caesar found an old travelogue of [[Stark]] describing trips to [[Esperanto]] and Windsurf, along with a framed rendering of the Carrot Cake midway showing [[Jack Harvey]]'s final achievements for [[The Carrot Cake]].

At the front of the train, the party confronted the train's vampire lord: a [[Vampiric Nightbringer]] with a faceless head, a screaming mouth, and white angelic wings. The nightbringer commanded spawn and familiars, drained Domyx, and tried to dominate Red Caesar. Red Caesar blasted out the front window with a Melf's Acid Arrow scroll, letting sunlight into the cabin. Ceril later used a Call Lightning scroll to tear open more of the train roof, bathing the nightbringer in sunlight and suppressing its regeneration.

Domyx struck the nightbringer hard enough to throw it from the train. The vampire dragged him under the racing cars, but Domyx caught a gap near the caboose and hauled himself back aboard.

### Chunk 0003

With the nightbringer weakened and exposed to sunlight, Vokenar broke its hold over Red Caesar, and the party finished the vampire. It disintegrated into pale feathers that turned black and blew out through the damaged train roof. The final lamp switch was now accessible.

Red Caesar examined the train controls, realized the train could be driven much faster or stopped, and accelerated it around its ocean loop. Once the party believed they had returned near the starting point, Red Caesar flipped the final switch. The fourth lamp lit, meaning the door to Jack Harvey's inner sanctum had opened back in the midway.

When the party returned to the paintings, the teleportation magic no longer worked. Red Caesar and Ceril determined that someone had used a high-level Dispel Magic to suppress the portal paintings, then left by casting Teleportation Circle. The party was trapped on Hole on Wheels overnight, far from the opened door, while the inner sanctum was exposed elsewhere in [[The Carrot Cake]]. The party took a long rest on the train.

During the rest, Red Caesar was forced into a magical version of a recurring [[Heaven's Bulb]] memory. He appeared as his current self alongside his younger self and [[Master Lee]]. [[Vizier Jade]] and [[Emperor Shen]] intruded into the dream, using Red Caesar's memories as a search space. Shen revealed too much in front of Red Caesar: the party's actions had granted access to the [[Penumbra]], and the Empire was searching while the party was incapacitated. Jade recognized that Red Caesar was aware inside his own dream. Shen threatened [[Obould]], saying the captive captain would not live to see another dawn.

After Jade and Shen moved on, Red Caesar cast True Seeing on his younger self. The younger Red Caesar saw through his future knowledge and identified a missing piece in Red Caesar's Penumbra work: Mending. This completed [[Obvolvo Caelum]], a spell that can condense Penumbra at any scale and smooth the conversion of [[Akasha]] into Penumbra.

### Summary

Key events:
- The party completed the fourth required [[King of the Hole]] wave and accepted the optional bonus challenge.
- The final King of the Hole victory lit the third lamp and awarded [[Domyx]] the [[Hopping Mad Sash]].
- [[Tango]] warned that [[The Opal]] was under [[Broyish Empire]] attack and that [[Kerben]] stayed behind to defend it.
- The party entered [[Hole on Wheels]], cleared the vampire-controlled train, defeated the [[Vampiric Nightbringer]], and lit the fourth lamp.
- The portal paintings back to the midway were dispelled, trapping the party on the train while [[Jack Harvey]]'s inner sanctum opened elsewhere.
- [[Vizier Jade]] and [[Emperor Shen]] invaded Red Caesar's dream, confirming the Empire was searching the opened [[Penumbra]] vault while the party rested.
- Red Caesar learned [[Obvolvo Caelum]], completing a major breakthrough in Penumbra condensation and [[Akasha]] synthesis.

Items and rewards:
- [[Hopping Mad Sash]] awarded to [[Domyx]] by the King of the Hole announcer.
- Preserved carrot tea recovered from Hole on Wheels.
- Scroll with two Mass Healing Word inscriptions recovered from Hole on Wheels.
- Old travelogue of [[Stark]], including [[Esperanto]] and Windsurf.
- Framed rendering of the Carrot Cake midway, depicting [[Jack Harvey]]'s final achievements for [[The Carrot Cake]].

The session pushed [[The Carrot Cake]] expedition from contained exploration into an urgent race. The party completed both remaining park challenges: [[King of the Hole]] was conquered through the required fourth wave and optional fifth challenge, and [[Hole on Wheels]] was cleared of a vampire lord that had taken over the train. All four lamps are now lit, meaning the way into [[Jack Harvey]]'s inner sanctum is open.

The victory came at a cost. The [[Broyish Empire]] attacked [[The Opal]], and [[Kerben]] remained away from the party to hold the ship. Someone with powerful dispelling magic then disabled the [[Hole on Wheels]] portal paintings after the fourth lamp was struck, trapping the party on the train overnight while the newly opened inner sanctum sat exposed. Red Caesar's dream confirmed that [[Vizier Jade]] and [[Emperor Shen]] are exploiting the party's incapacitation to search for the [[Penumbra]], and Shen directly threatened [[Obould]]'s life.

Red Caesar's magical breakthrough gives the party a new tool for the larger sky-repair problem. By incorporating Mending into his existing [[Akasha]]-to-[[Penumbra]] work, Red Caesar completed [[Obvolvo Caelum]], a spell that can condense Penumbra instantly and may help move or reshape it at the scale needed to repair the sky.
