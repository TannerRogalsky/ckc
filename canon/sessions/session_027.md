## Session 027

### Chunk 0000

The party arrived in [[Cutlass Cray]] after surviving a massive swell caused by the moon's destruction. [[The Marid]] had used his water genie magic to protect the town from the waves.

**Kerben** visited [[The Wonder Hulk]] and purchased three feather tokens: a swan boat, a swift multi-colored bird named Oscar, and a hot air balloon. He also acquired [[The Ascot]], Pipes of Smoke Monsters for the party, and three immovable rods. He traded his remaining gold and rare items for [[The Ascot]].

**Red Caesar** traded his Wand of Lightning Bolts, a sapphire, jade, and a mithril to The Wonder Hulk for the [[Wand of Blighting Bolts]], granting him access to necrotic damage for the first time. He also purchased a potion of invulnerability and a potion of feather fall.

**Vokenar** traded lapis lazuli to The Wonder Hulk for the [[Moon Blade]], a versatile warhammer fashioned by [[The Church of the Thirty Lights]] from a fragment of [[Crone]].

**Domyx** stocked up on protective and mobility potions before the expedition.

**Ceril** visited [[Gilder Savar]], a lich operating a potion shop. Ceril did not recognize him, but Gilder recognized Ceril from their shared escape from [[Brimbolyn]] alongside [[Vanzia Vinfei]] decades ago. Gilder chose undeath to "live long enough to perfect his craft." Ceril purchased protective and emergency-use supplies.

**The Opal** was fully repaired and upgraded with a new steering mechanism and a new crow's nest.

The party swung by the hidden grotto and confirmed [[The Hideous Truth]] was still concealed in the bay, now more overgrown. They left it as-is for additional camouflage.

The party set sail for [[The Carrot Cake]], a three-day journey. Kerben guided the way — this was the culmination of breadcrumbs he had followed since the first dungeon.

The entrance is a man-made lagoon carved into the mountainside, hidden by illusions that had only recently dissipated. The approach winds through rocky peaks and a colorful overgrown canopy with flowers of multiple seasons growing simultaneously. A glowing orange path leads up the mountain toward a giant carrot-shaped entrance marked with the "40 Carats" logo — a stylized carrot-diamond hybrid.

Inside the entrance corridor, illusory bands playing a jazzy waltz hover in the air. Tattered tents and horse hooks line the path. A [[Magen]] in rabbit form sat on a bench drinking tea from a fine white saucer. It welcomed the party: "come one, come all and welcome to the 40 carats, let the dance begin." The [[Magen]] told the undead to fight on their own.

As the party advanced, undead in tattered orange uniforms — mummies and revenants — rose from the ground around them. The party defeated them; the mummies retreated into the mud and the revenants were killed.

### Chunk 0001

The [[Magen]] confirmed that [[Penumbra]] is the power source still running the midway and provided a directory of the park's areas accessible through teleport circles: the [[Magic Hat]] (a hotel and bunker), the [[Hole Shebang]] (a festival island), and other zones. It explained [[Jack Harvey]] designed the park as a constellation of resorts across all of Stark.

The party explored the midway boulevard, a tree-lined path with specimens from across Stark — each fruiting with varieties that should not grow together. [[Ceril]] collected seeds from these trees. Stalls lined the path, some powered and some empty.

The party was ambushed by a [[Cloaker]] descending from the trees, two [[Flame Skull]]s hidden in a stall, and [[Hooksy the Clown Automaton]] animating from a pile of scrap. The cloaker had summoned the others through infrasound. [[Hooksy the Clown Automaton]] was revealed to be a living creature beneath metal plating rather than a true construct. The party defeated all three enemies. [[Red Caesar]] salvaged Hooksy's blood-covered clown mask.

[[Kerben]] played the "Mind the Mimic" carnival shell game, tracking treasure chests through increasingly complex shuffling rounds and winning gold and platinum carrot coins.

A haunted living tent attacked the party as they approached a large main tent.

### Chunk 0002

The party defeated the [[Haunted Living Tent]], a gargantuan creature that siphoned victims into an extra-dimensional tea cozy house where animated furniture attacked them. Inside, a [[Magen]] sat drinking tea. The tent was killed by sustained damage from both inside and out, eventually deflating and disintegrating into starry particles. [[Red Caesar]] and [[Kerben]] recovered [[Jack Harvey's Portrait]] from inside before the space collapsed.

Beyond the tent, the party entered a large courtyard with a sealed gate bearing the "40 Carats" logo and warding sigils. Four holes in the ground served as portals, each marked with a themed sign: the [[Magic Hat]], the [[Hole Shebang]], [[King of the Hole]], and [[Hole on Wheels]]. A [[Magen]] explained that each portal leads to a different area of the park and that four lamps must be lit to access [[Jack Harvey]]'s inner sanctum.

[[Red Caesar]] impulsively jumped into the Hole Shebang portal first. The rest of the party followed after [[Kerben]] warded the entrance and [[Domyx]] found a dead adventurer's belongings, including a [[Ranger Scroll]].

The party emerged from barrels in a small cavern called [[City Hole]], lit by electrically powered copper lanterns. Barrel portals here connect to the other zones. A wireframe gate blocks the path deeper into the Hole Shebang. The chunk ended with the party preparing to explore.

### Summary

The party spent a full shopping spree in [[Cutlass Cray]], significantly upgrading their capabilities before embarking on the three-day sail to [[The Carrot Cake]]. [[Kerben]]'s acquisition of [[The Ascot]] gives him powerful animal-swap utility. [[Red Caesar]] gained necrotic damage for the first time. [[Vokenar]] received a weapon directly tied to [[Crone]]'s remains. [[Ceril]]'s encounter with [[Gilder Savar]] revealed a personal connection to his pre-Cataclysm past. The party finally arrived at [[The Carrot Cake]] — the culmination of Kerben's long trail of breadcrumbs — and were welcomed by a [[Magen]] of [[Jack Harvey]]. After defeating undead in the entrance corridor, they explored the midway, learning the park's layout and discovering new threats including a [[Cloaker]], [[Flame Skull]]s, and [[Hooksy the Clown Automaton]]. They then defeated a [[Haunted Living Tent]] that trapped them in an extra-dimensional space, and discovered the central hub with four portal holes leading to different park zones. [[Red Caesar]] impulsively entered the [[Hole Shebang]] first, and the party followed, emerging in a cavern of barrel portals.
