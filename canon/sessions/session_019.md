## Events

### Chunk 0000

- [[Kerben]] used the [[Mangonel]] to launch [[Domyx]] across an 80-foot gap to the southern island platform of [[Castle Kaedon]].
- On the southern island, the party found angelic statues honoring aasimar heroes before the statues animated as marble gargoyles.
- The party encountered a [[Zorn]], a subterranean elemental creature composed of stone and soil. It had been eating gold coins and gems beneath the flagstones.
- The Zorn spoke Common and referred to its home as "axis monday." It feared [[Akasha]], which dematerializes elemental matter.
- Under pressure from [[Vokunar]]'s Akasha-infused weapon and [[Domyx]]'s intimidation, the Zorn vomited up roughly 50 pounds of gold coins before burrowing deep into the cliff face and fleeing.
- [[Ceril]]'s Plant Growth caused seaweed and fungus to overgrow the area, creating natural bridges and climbing supports between the broken island platforms.
- The party entered an acid-smoothed interior room where black puddings and [[Severed Hands]] emerged from the ruins.

### Chunk 0001

- The party finished clearing the acid-damaged room, then took a short rest before opening the arcane-locked door into the castle proper.
- In the entrance foyer, the party found a [[Flabbergast]] undead creature crushed beneath a portcullis and hidden under a red carpet.
- Despite being pinned, the Flabbergast tore itself free and attacked alongside a [[Carrion Crawler]], [[Severed Hands]], and giant scorpions.
- After the fight, part of the party traveled to [[The White Drake]], headquarters of [[The League of New Stark]], using Red Caesar's Teleportation Circle spell.

### Chunk 0002

- The party stayed overnight at [[The White Drake]] and attended a banquet. [[Vokunar]] sampled new foods, [[Domyx]] played Titan's Tarot with a lively crew, and [[Red Caesar]] sought out [[Lady Jacinthe]] for a private conversation.
- Jacinthe revealed she and [[Obould]] were once to be wed — not for romance, but for shared ambition. Jacinthe's ambition was building the League to unite kingdoms after the world was torn down; Obould's was adventure and piracy. Their diverging paths forced them apart.
- Jacinthe revealed that Obould gave her an oath: should she come to harm, he will go in her place and die first. He cursed himself with the oath and she cursed him with sorrow.
- [[Red Caesar]] learned of [[Starfall]], an ancient cannon of celestial origin now in the possession of the [[Broyish Empire]].
- Jacinthe explained the League's ultimate purpose: to give the world over to free peoples once the empire and other mounting forces are neutralized.
- The party returned to [[Castle Kaedon]] the next day during a storm. [[Ceril]] protected Red Caesar from the Mark of the Home curse, then used Greater Restoration to free [[Kerben]] after the curse took hold.
- In a pentagonal inner chamber, magic circles summoned [[Bearded Devil]]s, setting up the next battle.

## Summary

The party continued their exploration of [[Castle Kaedon]], pushing deeper into the risen structure. [[Kerben]] repurposed the [[Mangonel]] — previously a celebration device — as a transport mechanism, launching [[Domyx]] across an 80-foot gap to the southern island platform. The party discovered angelic statues honoring aasimar heroes, then fought the animated marble guardians and a [[Zorn]] that had been feasting on gold coins hidden beneath the flagstones. The creature's fear of [[Akasha]] proved exploitable, and it surrendered 50 pounds of gold coins before fleeing.

[[Ceril]]'s Plant Growth permanently changed the terrain, causing seaweed and fungus to form natural bridges and climbing supports between the broken platforms. The party then explored an acid-smoothed interior room where black puddings and [[Severed Hands]] emerged, followed by the castle's entrance foyer. A [[Flabbergast]] hidden beneath a portcullis and red carpet ambushed them alongside a [[Carrion Crawler]], [[Severed Hands]], and giant scorpions.

After the castle exploration, part of the party used Teleportation Circle to visit [[The White Drake]] for rest and resupply. [[Red Caesar]]'s private conversation with [[Lady Jacinthe]] revealed the deep personal history between her and [[Obould]] — a broken engagement rooted in diverging ambitions, sealed by a mutual oath and curse. Jacinthe also confirmed the [[Broyish Empire]] possesses [[Starfall]], an ancient cannon that fell from the heavens, and clarified the League's ultimate goal of freeing the world from imperial domination.

The next day, the party returned to [[Castle Kaedon]] during a storm and entered deeper into the castle. A seven-pointed heraldic seal triggered the Mark of the Home curse; [[Ceril]] shielded Red Caesar from it, then removed the curse from [[Kerben]] after it took hold. In the next pentagonal chamber, magic circles summoned [[Bearded Devil]]s, setting up the next confrontation.

## Connections

- The [[Zorn]]'s hoarding of gold coins suggests Castle Kaedon's treasures have been looted over time by subterranean creatures, not just by the party.
- The magic circles summoning [[Bearded Devil]]s connect to the fiendish presence [[Alamar]] sensed pervading the castle in session 015 — curses from hell and undead.
- The revelation about Obould and Jacinthe's past adds depth to [[The League of New Stark]]'s founding and explains Obould's personal investment in the League's survival.
- [[Starfall]] connects to the [[Broyish Empire]]'s pursuit of [[Penumbra]]-like stones, suggesting they seek to power this celestial weapon.
