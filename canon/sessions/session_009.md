# Session 009

## Events

- **[[The Pit]]:** The party arrives at the dwarvish settlement in the northeastern dwarven continent.
- **[[Figma Brickfinger's Union]]:** The Pitman's Union runs [[The Palace of the Pit]]. Led by [[Figma Brickfinger]].
- **[[The Deepworlders Delve]]:** Home of the Duergar, led by [[Morel Chainsunder]]. The Duergar are obsessed with the [[Penumbra]] and are culty and insular.
- **[[The Darvenlast]]:** A self-sufficient area within The Deepworlder's Dig. Entrance via a very thin break in a very thin mountain in the Dwarfish highlands, near [[The Funnel]].
- **[[The Funnel]]:** The primary mining operation area.
- **[[Sigrid Forgewelt]]:** A dwarven weapon forger in The Pit.
- **[[Beryzoz Helmscar]]:** A gnomish armor forger. Last issued visas to the [[Gheister]]. [[Red Caesar]]'s dwarvish visa was stolen here.
- **[[Otto]]'s friends:** The party's quest to find Otto's dwarf friends leads them here.

## Characters & Plot Summary

The party arrives at [[The Pit]], establishing the dwarven storyline. [[Figma Brickfinger's Union]] has an uneasy truce with the Duergar of [[The Deepworlders Delve]], though there was war in the past. The Duergar's obsession with the [[Penumbra]] connects the dwarven arc to the party's main quest. [[Red Caesar]]'s stolen visa from [[Beryzoz Helmscar]] is a personal stake. This session sets up the [[Darvinblast]] dungeon crawl.

## Connections

- Connects [[Otto]]'s recruitment quest to the dwarven storyline
- The Duergar's obsession with Penumbra ties the dwarven arc to the main quest
- Sets up the [[Darvinblast]] exploration in future sessions
